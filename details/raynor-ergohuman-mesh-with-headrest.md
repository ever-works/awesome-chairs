## Overview

The Raynor Ergohuman Mesh (model ME7ERG) is an all-mesh office chair with headrest that has been on the market for over a decade. It features a high backrest up to 30.5 inches tall and is designed as a one-size-fits-all option with similar functionality to premium chairs at a lower price.

## Features

- Adjustable height backrest (up to 30.5" tall)
- Height adjustable headrest with pivot function
- Padded mesh headrest design matching the chair's mesh construction
- Independent lumbar support section designed to flex based on user weight
- Seat slider for depth adjustment
- Tilt lock mechanism
- Tension adjustment
- Adjustable arms

## Potential Considerations

- The seat has a noticeable frame on the sides, though Raynor includes foam padding at the front to reduce discomfort
- The backrest does not go fully upright; it remains at a slight recline even at its most forward angle

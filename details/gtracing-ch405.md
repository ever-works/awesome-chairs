## Overview

The CH405 bridges gaming and office use with cooling-focused design for all-day sitting.

## Features

- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- Recline: 3-stage (up to 155°)
- Armrests: 3D adjustable (height, depth, angle)
- Material: Premium breathable mesh back and seat
- Lumbar support: Adjustable pillow
- Thermal regulation for long gaming/programming sessions

## Use Cases

- Programmers: Ergonomic hybrid for coding marathons
- Streamers: Comfortable positioning for broadcasts

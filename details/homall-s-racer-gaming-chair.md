## Overview

The Homall S-Racer is a budget racing-style gaming chair designed for entry-level users who need basic gaming-chair features and aesthetics at an affordable price point.

## Features

- **Racing-Style Design**: Classic sporty aesthetic for gaming setups
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Easy-to-clean, budget-friendly surface
- **Removable Cushions**: Lumbar and neck pillow support
- **Fixed Armrests**: Non-adjustable padded arms
- **Recline Function**: Up to 130° tilt
- **Height Adjustment**: Standard pneumatic gas lift
- **Swivel Base**: 360° rotation with smooth-rolling casters

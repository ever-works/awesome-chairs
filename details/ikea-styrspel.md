## Overview

The IKEA STYRSPEL is an entry-level gaming chair from IKEA's UPPSPEL gaming furniture collection. It was tested by Wirecutter and found to have several significant design flaws that made it unsuitable for recommendation.

## Design

- Fixed non-adjustable armrests
- Star-shaped protruding wheelbase
- PU leather or similar surface material

## Criticisms

Per Wirecutter testing:
- **Armrests**: Too wide for comfortable positioning during use
- **Wheelbase**: The protruding, star-shaped design makes it too easy to knock your ankles against it
- **Surface material**: Looks and feels like outdoor patio chair plastic, lacking the quality expected for extended sitting
- **Overall**: Not recommended compared to other options in the budget gaming chair category

## Price

Budget tier, sold through IKEA's standard pricing structure.

## Availability

Available at IKEA stores and on IKEA's website.

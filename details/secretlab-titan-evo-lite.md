# Secretlab Titan Evo Lite

## Overview
The Secretlab Titan Evo Lite is a more affordable gaming chair that retains the core ergonomic design of the Titan Evo series. It features built-in dynamic lumbar support, a memory foam head pillow, and cold-cure foam cushioning, making it suitable for long gaming or office sessions.

## Features
- Built-in dynamic lumbar support
- Memory foam neck pillow (magnetically attached)
- Cold-cure foam cushioning
- 4D adjustable armrests with Soft PU coated armrest tops
- Upholstery options: Secretlab Prime Leatherette, SoftWeave Fabric
- Two sizes: Regular (5’7”–6’2”), XL (5’11”–6’9”)
- Weight capacity: 220lbs (Regular), 395lbs (XL)
- Recline range: 85° to 165°
- Warranty: Up to 5 years

## Pricing
Approximately $399 for Prime Leatherette and $439 for SoftWeave Fabric (prices may vary by region and retailer).

## Use Cases
Ideal for users seeking a high-quality ergonomic chair with essential adjustments at a lower cost. Suitable for gaming, remote work, and general desk use.

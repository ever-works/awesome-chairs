## Overview

The Herman Miller Mirra 2 is an ergonomic task chair designed to provide responsive support that adapts to the user's movements while sitting. It combines adjustable ergonomic features with Herman Miller's signature build quality and design.

## Features

- PostureFit SL adjustable lumbar support
- TriFlex polymer backrest for flexible support
- Seat depth adjustment
- Adjustable arms
- Tilt limiter with seat angle control
- Pneumatic height adjustment
- Harmonic 2 tilt mechanism

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: 350 lbs
- Seat Height: 16\"–20.5\"
- Recline Range: Flexible tilt
- Warranty: 12-year Herman Miller warranty

## Pricing

Starting at approximately $1,115 for the standard model.

## Overview

The Neutral Posture Pillow Top is one of the largest ergonomic office chairs available, with a 27" wide seat and 500 lb. capacity. It combines high-end ergonomic features with a spacious design for big and tall users. Made in the USA.

## Key Specifications

- **Weight Capacity:** 500 lbs.
- **Seat Width:** 27" wide
- **Cylinder Options:** 3" tall (16"–19") or 5" tall (18"–23")
- **Starting Price:** ~$2,828.99

## Features

- Multi-function mechanism including seat depth, seat angle, back height, and independent back angle adjustment
- Two cylinder options to accommodate tall individuals up to 7 feet
- 33 fabric and 12 leather upholstery options
- All leather options are top grain, high-end quality
- Extra-wide 27" seat for maximum space
- Pillow Top Junior version available in 23" wide option for narrower requirements

## Best For

Big and tall users seeking the highest level of ergonomic adjustability combined with a spacious seat. Ideal for users up to 7 feet tall who need a taller cylinder option.

## Considerations

- Massive size means it won't fit all spaces or all body types
- Not returnable due to upholstery customization
- Color swatches are available to verify colors before ordering

## Overview

Salli is the market leader in the saddle chair category, offering ergonomic seating that encourages users to sit in an upright position similar to horseback riding. This positions the pelvis in a neutral posture with the spine naturally supporting the upper body.

## Features

- Split saddle seat design for additional health benefits and pressure distribution
- Tilt or 360-degree sway mechanism for increased movement
- Promotes optimal posture improvement with naturally induced proper spinal curvature
- Multiple accessories to adapt to any type of work
- Reduces pressure points compared to traditional flat seats
- Increases both unconscious and conscious movements while seated

## Use Cases

- Professionals who need to move around their workspace while seated upright
- Sit-to-stand desk users seeking ergonomic alternatives
- Healthcare and dental professionals (a core Salli market)
- Office workers looking to improve posture and reduce sedentary health risks

## Important Considerations

- Not compatible with regular desk height; requires sit-to-stand desks or adjustable-height work surfaces
- May take up to a couple of weeks to adapt to the different sitting style

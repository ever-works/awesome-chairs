## Overview

The Trust GXT 716 Rizza is a gaming chair designed for kids and young adults that integrates RGB LED lighting tubes along the outer edges. With over 350 customisable RGB modes and a striking black PU leather finish, this chair delivers a futuristic aesthetic. Despite the LED focus, it maintains solid basic gaming chair functionality with height-adjustable arm rests and neck/lumbar support.

## Features

- **RGB Lighting**: Integrated LED tubes along outer edges (not simple strips) with over 350 customisable modes
- **Controls**: Remote control and powerbank with dedicated storage pocket
- **Material**: Black PU leather seat with high-density foam padding, metal frame, plastic armrests
- **Lumbar Support**: Removable cushion for lumbar areas
- **Neck Support**: Removable cushion for neck areas
- **Armrests**: 2D adjustable armrests (height and rotation) — essential for avoiding arm, wrist, and shoulder strain
- **Seat Height**: Adjustable from 45–53 cm
- **Recline**: Reclining backrest up to 175° with tilt mechanism and lock
- **Casters**: Five black nylon wheels, suitable for various floor types

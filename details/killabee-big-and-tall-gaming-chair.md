## Overview

The Killabee Big and Tall Gaming Chair is a heavy-duty gaming chair designed specifically for larger users, featuring a 350 lbs weight capacity, massage lumbar support, and an extra-wide seat for superior comfort during extended gaming sessions.

## Features

- **Big and Tall Design**: Extra-wide seat and tall backrest
- **350 lbs Capacity**: Heavy-duty construction for larger users
- **Massage Lumbar Support**: USB-powered vibrating cushion
- **Retractable Footrest**: Built-in footrest for relaxation
- **Memory Foam Padding**: High-density foam for comfort
- **Durable Construction**: Reinforced frame and base

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 350 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 180°
- Seat Width: Extra-wide for comfort
- Power: USB-powered massage

## Overview

The N-Gen Gaming Chair is a budget-oriented gaming chair that was evaluated in Wirecutter's gaming chair guide and found to be lacking in several key ergonomic areas.

## Design

- Very short backrest providing limited upper body support
- No headrest support
- No recline function
- Fixed armrests that are wide, low, and non-adjustable

## Criticisms

Per Wirecutter testing:
- **No headrest**: Lacks any form of head or neck support
- **No recline**: Backrest is fixed in place with no tilting or reclining capability
- **Short backrest**: The backrest height is insufficient for adequate support
- **Armrests**: Wide, low, and completely non-adjustable, making them impractical for most users
- **Ergonomics**: Not suitable for extended sitting sessions due to the lack of adjustability

## Overview

The Hookay Elevate ELMF01 is built for all-day comfort with a focus on spinal health and adaptability.

## Features

- Adaptive Spinal Flex Support system that follows natural spine movement
- Dual-zone lumbar support with adjustable height and firmness
- Adjustable seat height and depth
- 2D adjustable headrest (height and angle)
- 360° rotating armrests with expandable width
- Tilt lock and tension control
- Forward tilt for active sitting
- Premium breathable mesh backrest
- High-density molded foam seat cushion
- BIFMA certified, expected lifespan of 10 years
- Modern design in black, white, and orange

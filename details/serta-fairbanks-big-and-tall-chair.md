## Overview

The Serta Fairbanks seamlessly blends office aesthetics with gaming comfort, making it ideal for hybrid work-hard, play-hard lifestyles. Built for larger users, it offers generous seating dimensions and plush cushioning for all-day comfort.

## Specifications

| Feature | Details |
|---------|--------|
| Weight Capacity | 350 lbs |
| Height Recommendation | 5'6\" — 6'6\" |
| Seat Width | 22 inches |
| Recline Range | 90° — 130° |
| Material | Bonded Leather |
| Armrests | Fixed, Upholstered |
| Lumbar Support | Contoured Lumbar Zone |

## Features

- **350-lb Weight Capacity**: Supports bigger frames without feeling cramped
- **22-inch Wide Seat**: Generous seating width for larger builds
- **Bonded Leather Upholstery**: Sleek, professional look that elevates any workspace
- **Deep Plush Cushioning**: Thick padding for extended comfort without fatigue
- **Contoured Lumbar Zone**: Built-in lower back support to reduce strain during long sessions
- **Elevated Headrest**: Neck support designed for taller users
- **Fixed Upholstered Armrests**: Sculpted for comfort in a permanent position
- **130° Recline**: Switches between focused work and relaxed downtime
- **Dual-Wheel Casters**: Smooth gliding across floors
- **Versatile Design**: Functions equally well as an office chair and gaming chair

## Build Quality

- Sturdy steel frame construction
- Hefty and solidly built for stability
- Dual-wheel caster system for mobility
- Designed for durability and long-term use

Mid-range pricing reflecting its dual-purpose office and gaming functionality, with competitive value for its build quality and comfort features.

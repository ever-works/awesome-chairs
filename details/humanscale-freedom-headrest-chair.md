# Humanscale Freedom Headrest Chair

An ergonomic desk chair designed for all-day computer use, especially suited to people who prefer to recline while working.

## Features
- **Weight-sensitive recline**: Adjusts tension automatically based on body weight
- **Dynamic headrest**: Moves as you recline to support head and neck
- **Pivoting backrest on rails**: Tracks spinal movement
- **Eco-friendly materials**: Sustainably constructed
- **Seat adjustment**: Height and forward/backward slide

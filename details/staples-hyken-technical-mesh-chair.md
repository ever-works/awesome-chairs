## Overview

The Staples Hyken Technical Mesh Chair is a popular mesh task chair known for excellent value and comprehensive adjustability. It features breathable mesh construction throughout with adjustable lumbar support at a competitive price point.

## Features

- **Full Mesh Construction**: Breathable mesh seat and back
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable lower back positioning
- **Adjustable Headrest**: Removable and adjustable neck support
- **Height-Adjustable Arms**: Customizable arm positioning
- **Tilt Function**: Multi-position tilt lock
- **Popular Value Choice**: Well-reviewed for price-to-feature ratio

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 250 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 115°
- Seat Height Range: 17.5" to 21.5"
- Warranty: Limited manufacturer warranty
- Available Colors: Black and silver

## Overview

The Nouhaus Palette features adjustable lumbar support combined with 135-degree 2:1 Synchro Tilt that tilts both the back and seat together. This chair is perfect for shorter people and features a compact size with rich vibrant colors.

## Features

- **Just-For-Me Lumbar Adjust**: Customizable lower back support
- **2:1 Synchro Tilt**: Synchronized seat and backrest movement at 2:1 ratio
- **135° Recline**: Generous recline range for comfort
- **Height Variable Armrests**: Adjustable arm support with Soft-Grip PU
- **Compact Design**: Optimized for users under 5'8"
- **Vibrant Color Options**: Available in multiple bold colors

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 240 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 135°
- Ideal Height Range: 4'11" to 5'8"
- Warranty: 2-year manufacturer warranty

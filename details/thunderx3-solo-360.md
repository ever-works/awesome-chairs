## Overview

The ThunderX3 Solo 360 is a budget-friendly gaming chair praised for its well-rounded performance, combining comfort, adjustability, and a subtle design.

## Specifications

- Seat type: Task chair
- Weight capacity: 136 kg (300 lbs)
- Warranty: 1 year

## Features

- 360-degree adjustable armrests (3D x 360)
- Synchronous tilt for recline with seat adjustment
- Adjustable headrest (up/down)
- Firm foam cushioning with breathable materials: multi-layer fabric (Loft Air), mesh, or leatherette
- Generous recline with lockable positions
- Easy assembly for one person

## Pricing

Positioned as a very affordable option in the budget category.

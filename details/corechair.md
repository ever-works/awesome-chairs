## Overview

The CoreChair is an ergonomic chair designed to improve posture through gentle movement. It is engineered to promote natural posture correction rather than simply supporting a static, slumped position.

## Design Philosophy

- Uses dynamic movement to help users build stronger postural muscles over time
- Unlike traditional ergonomic chairs that lock users into one position, the CoreChair encourages natural micro-movements
- Sway-based mechanism allows gentle pivoting while seated, maintaining an active, engaged sitting posture

## Comfort and Adjustability

- Adjustable seat height
- Tension adjustment to control the responsiveness of the sway mechanism
- Pelvic stabilizer built into the seat design
- Designed to prevent the user from sliding forward

## User Experience

- Some users may experience initial discomfort during adaptation as core and postural muscles engage and strengthen
- Once adapted, users report reduced tendency to slouch and improved overall posture
- Particularly effective for users who find they constantly slump in traditional chairs

## Best For

- Users who want to actively improve their posture rather than passively sit
- Office workers who spend significant time seated
- People looking to reduce the health impacts of static sitting

## Overview

The OM Yes is an ergonomic office chair by OmniHuman designed with a highly adjustable headrest system. It is positioned in the $700 price range for chairs with quality headrests.

## Headrest Features

- 3-way adjustable headrest for precise positioning
- Flatter headrest design that can lay against the head or neck
- Bonus back height adjustment allows moving the headrest up and down to fit the user's preferred position
- Headrest can be positioned to accommodate different sitting postures

## Additional Features

- Reclining capability
- Designed for extended sitting comfort
- Adjustable back height complements the headrest adjustment range

## Pricing

Approximately $700.

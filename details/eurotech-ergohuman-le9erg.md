## Overview

The Eurotech Ergohuman LE9ERG is an ergonomic office chair offered in multiple upholstery configurations: full mesh, leather and mesh combination, or all-leather. It is widely available through office chair retailers and features robust lumbar support paired with an adjustable headrest.

## Headrest Assessment

- **Tier Rank:** C- (average across reviewers)
- **Adjustment:** Height adjustable with pivot; easier to adjust than some competitors but less solid
- **Comfort Issues:**
  - The headrest is considered too aggressive, especially in the leather version, constantly pushing the head forward
  - Tends to slide down during use, particularly when reclining, requiring frequent readjustment
  - Leather version feels warm and can cause discomfort during extended use
  - The aggressive lumbar support combined with the aggressive headrest makes it difficult to find a comfortable position
- **Reviewer Consensus:** Most reviewers rated it C- to D-tier, noting it works better than many lower-ranked options but falls short of being genuinely comfortable or recommended

## Features

- Multiple upholstery options: mesh, leather-mesh combo, and all-leather
- Aggressive lumbar support design that pushes the lower back forward
- Adjustable headrest with height and pivot adjustment
- Designed to pitch the head forward for monitor focus during tasking

## Features

- Seat height adjustment suitable for users from 5'5" to over 6'2"
- 4-way adjustable armrests (height, width, depth, rotation; height range somewhat limited)
- Smooth recline mechanism with generous range and 4 locking positions, including fully upright
- Height-adjustable lumbar support
- 2-way adjustable headrest
- Mesh backrest with open design, no restrictive bolsters
- Soft lumbar pad
- Soft foam at seat front, but firmer toward middle and back

## Adjustments

Numerous adjustments for the price point, lacking seat depth adjustment.

## Pricing

$300-$400.

## Overview

FlexiSpot XRC5 offers multi-zone massage and heat targeted at gaming fatigue recovery, with recliner design for extended lounging.

## Features

- Four-zone massage with multiple modes
- Built-in heat function
- Rocker and 360° swivel base
- Oversized cushioned proportions

## Use Cases

- Gamers/streamers taking recovery breaks in reclined position
- Large/tall users in casual gaming setups

## Pros

- Customizable massage/heat for back relief
- Versatile motion for comfort

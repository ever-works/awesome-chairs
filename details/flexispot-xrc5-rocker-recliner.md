## Overview

The FlexiSpot XRC5 Rocker Recliner is a gaming recliner designed for comfort and relaxation. Known for ergonomic products, FlexiSpot extends that reputation to their recliner line with this feature-rich model.

## Features

- Oversized design that accommodates taller and bigger users comfortably
- Built-in heat function for warmth during gaming or lounging sessions
- Four massage zones with multiple massage modes for customizable comfort
- Swivel and rocking action for flexible positioning
- Cushioned construction that balances softness with adequate support
- Suitable for both gaming use and general relaxation after a long day

## Pros and Cons

- Spacious design fits larger body types well
- Heat and massage features add comfort beyond basic seating
- Sturdy rocking and swivel mechanisms
- The process to turn on heat without massage could be more streamlined

## Design

The XRC5 features a sleek recliner aesthetic that fits well in a gaming space or living room. The oversized proportions ensure comfort for users of various sizes.

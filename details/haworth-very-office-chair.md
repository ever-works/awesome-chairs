## Haworth Very Office Chair

### Overview
The **Haworth Very Office Chair** is an ergonomic task chair designed for active, dynamic sitting in modern work environments.

### Features
- **Responsive flex back**: Integrated lumbar support that follows body movement
- **Breathable mesh construction**: Temperature regulation for all-day comfort
- **Adjustable headrest**: Height and angle for neck support
- **Synchronized tilt mechanism**: Multiple lock positions for recline control
- **Adjustable arms**: Height and width control

### Specifications
- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- Warranty: Haworth standard warranty

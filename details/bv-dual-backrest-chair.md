## Overview

The BV Dual Backrest Chair is a more niche office chair priced at $229.99 on Amazon. It is a more affordable alternative to the premium DuoRest chair, featuring a unique split backrest design.

## Dual Backrest Design

- Unique split backrest allows for freedom of movement and flexibility
- Supports dynamic sitting position
- May feel like a lack of support for users who prefer traditional, firm backrests
- Some users may feel like they have to "hold themselves up"

## Seat

- Seat is on the firmer side
- May work well for users who prefer firmer seating

## Adjustments

- Arms have decent adjustment range
- Movement is not the smoothest on the arm sliders
- Arm caps are somewhat firm

## Headrest

- Good quality headrest with decent padding
- Comfortable and adjustable positioning

## Target Users

This chair is best suited for a specific type of buyer who prefers:
- Dynamic, flexible backrest over rigid support
- Freedom of movement while seated
- An affordable dual-backrest option

## Pricing

- Price: $229.99
- Available on Amazon

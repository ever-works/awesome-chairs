## Overview

The Lamia Chair is a mid-back ergonomic office chair with mesh construction and adjustable features for comfortable desk seating.

## Features

- Breathable mesh back
- Adjustable armrests (height and angle)
- Synchro-tilt mechanism
- Pneumatic height adjustment
- Compact design for smaller workspaces

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 250 lbs
- Recline Range: Flexible tilt with tension control

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing for compact ergonomic seating.

## Overview

The Steelcase Series 1 is the entry-level chair in Steelcase's ergonomic lineup, providing core ergonomic features at an accessible price point while maintaining Steelcase build quality standards.

## Features

- **Four-way adjustable arms** (height, width, depth, pivot)
- **Weight-activated tilt mechanism**
- **Quality frame construction** with well-finished molds and stitching
- **Variety of upholstery options**

## Pricing

2026 Price: ~$507.00

## Sizing Recommendations

- Suitable for small to medium builds
- Not recommended for users over 6'3" or heavier than 250–275 lbs

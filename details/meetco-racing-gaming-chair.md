## Overview
Meet&Co Racing Gaming Chair is a top pick for gamers seeking a blend of ergonomic support and racing-inspired aesthetics.

## Features
- Racing-style wrapped backrest for posture support
- Detachable headrest for neck support
- 4D armrests (up/down, forward/backward, side-to-side, rotation)
- Smooth hydraulic height adjustment
- Adjustable recline tension and lock
- Sturdy base with smooth-rolling casters

## Use Cases
Ideal for extended gaming sessions, office work, and anyone needing adjustable lumbar and neck support.

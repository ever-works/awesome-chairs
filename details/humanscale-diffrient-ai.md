## Overview

The Diffrient AI is the most adjustable chair ever created by Humanscale, combining automatic tilt responsiveness with the customization options typically found in manual-adjustment premium chairs.

## Features

- **FormMotion auto-tilt mechanism**: Automatically adjusts recline tension to user weight
- **Adjustable recline range and tension**: Override auto-tension as desired
- **Adjustable mesh backrest**: Height and depth adjustable
- **4D armrests**: Adjustable height, width, depth, and pivot
- **Seat depth slider**: Adjust forward and backward
- **Breathable mesh construction**: Tri-panel back design
- **Weight-activated recline**: No knobs or levers required for tilt balance

## Adjustability

- Recline tension (override auto-tilt): Yes
- Recline range: Adjustable
- Seat depth: Slider adjustment
- Back height: Adjustable
- Back depth: Adjustable
- Arm height: Adjustable
- Arm width: Adjustable
- Arm depth: Adjustable
- Arm pivot: Adjustable

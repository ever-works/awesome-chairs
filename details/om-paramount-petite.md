## Overview

The Paramount Petite by Office Master is one of the most adjustable petite office chairs available at a budget-friendly price point. It features some of the smallest dimensions in its class and is assembled in the U.S. from parts sourced in China and Taiwan.

## Dimensions

- Minimum seat height: 15.5 inches
- Minimum arm height: 5.25 inches
- Minimum arm width: 13.25 inches
- Minimum seat depth: 16 inches

## Features

- Center tilting ability with tension control
- Adjustable seat depth
- Adjustable seat angle with forward seat tilt
- Adjustable backrest angle
- Adjustable back height
- Two-way arm option
- Seven-year warranty with five years of coverage on foam and fabric

## Pricing

Starting at $513.99 (2026).

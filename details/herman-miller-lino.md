## Overview

The Herman Miller Lino is Herman Miller's most affordable ergonomic office chair, offering the brand's renowned build quality and design at an accessible price point.

## Features

- Breathable suspension back with integrated lumbar support
- Adjustable seat depth
- Adjustable arms (height and angle)
- Comfortable cushioned seat
- Minimal aesthetic design
- Pneumatic height adjustment

## Specifications

- Price range: $670–$855
- Weight Capacity: Up to 300 lbs
- Recline Range: Up to 15°
- Warranty: 12-year Herman Miller warranty
- Refurbished available from $240

## Pricing

Starting at $670–$855 for new models.

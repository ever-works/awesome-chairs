## Overview

The Hinomi H2 Pro is a mesh gaming chair designed for intense desk use with exceptional adjustability and versatility. It improves significantly on its predecessor, the H1 Pro, with better build quality, more adjustments, and refined features based on user feedback.

## Specifications

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Backrest Height | 24.4-33.9" |
| Seat Height | 18-22.8" |
| Seat Width | 20.8" |
| Seat Depth | 16.7-19.9" |
| Max Tilt | 136° |
| Max Load | 330 lbs |

## Key Features

- **19 adjustment points**: Height, seat depth, rocking tension, lumbar support, and neck rest are all fully adjustable
- **4-point headrest adjustment**: Adds depth and angle adjustment beyond typical up/down movement
- **5-point lumbar adjustment**: Provides excellent lumbar and thoracic support
- **6D armrests**: Full-dimensional movement including expanded height range and ability to tilt up and down; arms can fold completely up and out of the way
- **Foldable backrest**: Can be unlocked and folded down so the chair fits completely under a desk for space savings
- **Optional footrest**: Available as an add-on purchase (~$40), padded supports shift out of the way when not in use
- **Tilt angle and tension control**: Customizable rocking experience

## Pros

- Fantastic support via 19 different adjustments
- Most adjustable armrests available outside of premium office chairs like Steelcase Gesture
- Can tuck completely out of the way for space saving
- Much improved build quality compared to the original H1 Pro

## Cons

- The large number of adjustments takes time to dial in properly
- Doesn't accommodate cross-legged sitting posture

## Pricing

Pricing is competitive with the original H1 Pro, offering meaningful upgrades without a significant price increase. The optional footrest is approximately $40.

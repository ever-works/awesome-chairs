## Overview

DXRacer is a gaming chair brand that introduced the world's first gaming chair in 2006, known as the Formula Series. This marked a groundbreaking milestone in the gaming industry and established DXRacer's legendary status in the gaming chair market.

## Manufacturing & Quality

DXRacer chairs undergo a series of rigorous tests including:
- Stability testing
- Load-bearing testing
- Application testing

The company emphasizes building products that are durable and safe, with close attention to detail in every aspect of manufacturing.

## Product Range

DXRacer offers a range of products including:
- Ergonomic gaming chairs (multiple series including Formula, Blade, Craft, Master, KOI, and others)
- Desks
- Accessories

All products are designed to ensure comfort during long hours of gaming or working.

## History

Founded in 2006, DXRacer pioneered the gaming chair category with the Formula Series — the world's first chair specifically designed for gamers. The brand has since expanded its product lineup and maintains a presence in esports events and collaborations.

## Pricing

Pricing varies by product series and model. DXRacer chairs are positioned in the mid-to-premium range, with regular promotions and sales available.

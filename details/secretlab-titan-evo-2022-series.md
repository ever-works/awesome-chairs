## Overview

The Secretlab Titan Evo 2022 Series is our pick for the best gaming chair you can buy today. It's a relatively affordable chair, but it's rocking quality materials and great features that you would expect from something twice as expensive.

## Specifications

* **Upholstery:** Secretlab NEO Hybrid Leatherette, SoftWeave Plus Fabric, Secretlab NAPA Leather, or Exotic Ash Microsuede
* **Cushion:** cold-cure foam
* **Lumbar:** Built-in dynamic lumbar support
* **Backrest:** 33 inches
* **Seat width:** 19.3 inches
* **Recline:** 85 -165 degrees
* **Armrests:** Adjustable 4-directional armrests with hot-swappable armrest tops
* **Weight limit:** 200lbs (Small); 220lbs (Regular); 395lbs (XL)
* **User height range:** ≤ 5’6” (Small) / 5’7” - 6’2” (Regular) / 5’11” - 6’9” (XL)
* **Warranty:** Up to 5 years
* **Unique features:** Memory foam head pillow; optional SoftWeave Plus fabric skins

## Features

- Available in three sizes (Small, Regular, XL) to fit a wide variety of users
- Each size comes in four upholstery options
- Built-in lumbar support that automatically adjusts to your sitting position
- 4D adjustable armrests
- Memory foam neck pillow that attaches magnetically
- Cold-cure foam cushioning
- Thick, durable construction

## Pricing

- Leatherette models start at $429
- SoftWeave Plus editions start at $469
- NAPA Leather starts at $899
- Microsuede editions are $1,700
- Various color and theme options available, including skins based on games like League of Legends and Overwatch or franchises like Batman, Game of Thrones, and more

## Overview

The Sihoo C7 is an ergonomic office chair designed for comfort during meetings and focused work sessions. It features a breathable mesh back, an excellent adjustable headrest for breaks and calls, and soft padded armrests that adjust in three directions.

## Features

- Breathable mesh back that stays clean longer and reduces sweating
- Excellent headrest for leaning back during breaks or meetings
- Soft padded armrests adjustable in three directions: in/out, up/down, and left/right
- Lumbar support that moves in and out (but not up and down)
- Strong mesh support capable of supporting users of various weights
- Comfortable for prolonged sitting during video calls and meetings

## Lumbar Support

The lumbar support sits low to stabilize the pelvis, consistent with ergonomic design principles. It adjusts in and out but cannot be raised vertically to fit the curve of taller backs. This fixed vertical position may not suit everyone's preferences.

## Best Use Case

Ideal for users who spend significant time in meetings rather than typing all day. The headrest and comfortable armrests make leaning during calls particularly pleasant.

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing that offers good value for the features provided.

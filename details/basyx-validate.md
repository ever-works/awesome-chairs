## Overview

The Basyx Validate is a classic high back leather big and tall office chair with reinforced components throughout. It is designed to support users up to 450 lbs with a solid, durable build.

## Features

- 31 5/8" tall backrest
- 22" wide seat
- 450 lbs weight capacity
- SofThread leather upholstery with decorative stitching
- Thick seat and back padding
- Integrated headrest at top of backrest
- Padded loop arms
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment
- Center-tilt with tension control
- Lock in full upright position

## Limitations

- Large size not suitable for conference room applications with small to medium sized users
- One of the least adjustable chairs in its class
- Limited adjustability overall
- SofThread is vinyl, not real leather
- Takes up significant floor space

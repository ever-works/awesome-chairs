## Overview

The Varier Multi is a portable kneeling chair that strikes a balance between comfort, quality, and convenience. It is designed for occasional use and short periods of working (around 1-2 hours at a time).

## Features

- Foldable and portable design for easy storage
- Made to an exceptionally high quality standard
- 7-year warranty
- Promotes active sitting and proper posture during use
- Lightweight enough to carry and store away when not needed

## Best Use Case

The Multi is ideal for short sitting periods (up to 1-2 hours). For longer work sessions (beyond 2 hours), Varier recommends the Wing, Variable, Variable Plus, or Thatsit chairs instead.

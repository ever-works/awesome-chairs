## Overview

Amia uses LiveBack technology mimicking spine motion for dynamic support during extended sitting.

## Features

- LiveBack dynamic lumbar support
- Adjustable arms and tilt limiter
- Breathable mesh back
- Supports natural postures for coding and meetings

## Use Cases

- Programmer long-hours work
- Office and executive settings

## Comparisons

More professional and subtle than gaming chairs with equivalent or better adjustability.

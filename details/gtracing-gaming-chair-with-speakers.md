## Overview

The GTRACING Gaming Chair With Speakers is a popular runner-up choice for gamers seeking built-in audio. It features Bluetooth speakers in both shoulder wings that connect to devices in seconds, along with a smooth faux leather design available in multiple colors.

## Features

- Two Bluetooth speakers in the shoulder wings
- Quick and easy Bluetooth connectivity
- Smooth faux leather upholstery
- Thick padded seat cushion
- Retractable footrest
- Adjustable back support
- Strong metal frame
- Reclining backrest with tilt, lock, and recline controls
- Available in a variety of colors

## Specifications

- Dimensions: 20.87"D x 21.65"W x 53.15"H (53.01 cm x 54.99 cm x 135 cm)
- Speaker location: Both shoulder wings
- Connectivity: Bluetooth
- Comfort features: Smooth faux leather upholstery, reclining, padded seat cushion

## Drawbacks

- Stability concerns reported by some users
- Some durability concerns noted in reviews

## Overview

The Furmax Gaming Chair High Back is a budget-friendly racing gaming chair with ergonomic design and PU leather upholstery, offering solid construction and comfort for casual gamers at an accessible price point.

## Features

- **High-Back Design**: Full back support for gaming
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Durable and easy to maintain
- **Ergonomic Support**: Lumbar and neck pillows included
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Height-adjustable arms
- **Tilt Mechanism**: Reclining function with lock
- **Sturdy Construction**: Heavy-duty base and casters

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 280 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 135°
- Seat Height Range: 18" to 21.5"
- Warranty: 1-year manufacturer warranty
- Available Colors: Multiple options

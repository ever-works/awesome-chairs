## Overview

The Furmax Gaming Chair High Back is a budget-friendly racing gaming chair with ergonomic design and PU leather upholstery, offering solid construction and essential features for casual gamers at an accessible price point.

## Features

- **High-Back Design**: Full back support for gaming
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Durable and easy to maintain
- **Removable Cushions**: Lumbar and neck pilllows included
- **Fixed Armrests**: Non-adjustable padded arms
- **Reclining Function**: 90°–130° tilt range
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 275 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 130°
- Seat Height Range: 18" to 22"
- Warranty: 1-year limited warranty
- Available Colors: Multiple color options

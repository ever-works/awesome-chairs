## Overview

The Uplift Pursuit is an ergonomic office chair positioned at the higher end of the budget range at $499. It combines a breathable mesh backrest and headrest with a foam seat featuring a waterfall edge to reduce thigh pressure and improve leg circulation.

## Features

- Breathable mesh backrest and headrest
- High-density foam seat with waterfall edge for reduced pressure and better circulation
- Adjustable lumbar support with both vertical and depth adjustment (moves toward or away from the back)
- Numerous adjustment points throughout including unconventional pull-up/push-down armrest mechanism
- Small under-seat storage compartment for keys, wallet, or phone
- Unique and modern design aesthetic
- Available in black or white

## Specifications

- **Colors**: Black, white
- **Materials**: Mesh, foam
- **Seat width**: 18.5 inches
- **Weight limit**: 250 pounds

## Pricing

Retails for $499, positioned at the higher end of the budget office chair category.

## Considerations

- The 18.5-inch seat and 250-pound weight limit make it less ideal for larger or taller users
- Armrests adjust in an unconventional way (pulled up or pushed down) which may take some getting used to

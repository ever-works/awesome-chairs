## Overview

The Autonomous ErgoChair Mesh is the new and improved sibling to the ErgoChair Pro, featuring a redesigned mesh seat cushion for extra breathability. This updated version was reviewed in February 2025 and offers enhanced comfort for extended sitting.

## Features

- **Redesigned Mesh Seat**: Enhanced breathability compared to foam
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable lower back support
- **9 Adjustment Points**: Comprehensive ergonomic customization
- **Breathable Mesh Back**: Full ventilation for all-day comfort
- **Synchro-Tilt Mechanism**: Coordinated seat and backrest movement
- **Adjustable Headrest**: Removable and adjustable neck support

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 300 lbs
- Recline Range: Up to 18° tilt
- Seat Depth: Adjustable from 19\" to 21.5\"
- Warranty: 2-year warranty with 30-day returns

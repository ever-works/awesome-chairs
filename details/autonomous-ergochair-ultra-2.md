## Overview

The Autonomous ErgoChair Ultra 2 is a 3D-printed office chair with an all-mesh design. It is a significant improvement over the original ErgoChair Ultra, featuring a more comfortable seat that no longer feels flat and hard.

## Features

- 3D-printed construction
- All-mesh design for excellent airflow and breathability
- Adjustable seat depth
- Recline lock
- Wide backrest that does not dig into shoulders
- Adjustable armrests

## Considerations

- Armrests can sometimes go down with a loud crack when too much pressure is applied (e.g., when getting up or shifting weight), despite being locked
- Two-year warranty is considered lackluster for the price point
- Customer service quality has been reported as subpar by some users

## Pricing

Priced at $399. Comes with a two-year warranty.

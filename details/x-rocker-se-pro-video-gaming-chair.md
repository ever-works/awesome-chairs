## Overview

The X Rocker SE Pro Video Gaming Chair features a unique pedestal design with two shoulder wing speakers plus a larger subwoofer on the back for exceptional in-game audio immersion. It supports both Bluetooth and wired connections, making it compatible with a wide range of gaming devices.

## Features

- Two speakers in shoulder wings
- Rear-mounted subwoofer for enhanced bass
- Dual connectivity: Bluetooth or wired connection
- Built-in control panel on the chair for volume and bass adjustment
- Plush padding on back, seat, and armrests
- Foldable design for easy storage and transport
- Compatible with gaming consoles

## Specifications

- Dimensions: 30.7"D x 30.7"W x 39.76"H (78 cm x 78 cm x 101 cm)
- Speaker location: Both shoulder wings and a subwoofer on the back
- Connectivity: Bluetooth or wired connection
- Comfort features: Soft foam padding throughout, back support

## Drawbacks

- Higher price point compared to other gaming chairs with speakers
- No dedicated lumbar support pillow

## Pricing

More expensive than many competing options but offers premium audio with the added subwoofer.

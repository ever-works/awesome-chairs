## Overview

The Herman Miller Setu is a collaborative seating solution designed for agile environments. It features a Kinematic backrest that flexes to support the user's movement.

## Features

- Kinematic suspension back
- Tilt tension with tension adjustment
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment
- Fixed arm pads
- Lightweight construction
- Sustainable materials

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- Seat Height: 16.75"–21"
- Warranty: 12-year Herman Miller warranty

## Pricing

Starting at approximately $650.

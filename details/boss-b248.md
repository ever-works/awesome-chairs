## Overview

Budget kneeling chair with metal X-shaped base and four casters. Offers pneumatic seat height adjustment despite low-end build.

## Use Cases

- Versatile for mixed use as an active seating alternative in home or small offices.
- Good for posture breaks during prolonged desk work.

## Features

- Pneumatic height adjustment.
- Thin supportive padding on seat and knee pads.
- Strong warranty.

## Comparisons

- More portable than full active chairs, but lacks swivel/rocking motion.

## Pricing

Approximately $75.71.

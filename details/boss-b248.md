## Overview

The Boss B248 is a budget kneeling chair with a metal X-shaped base and four casters. Despite its low-end build quality, it offers pneumatic seat height adjustment and a strong warranty, making it a reasonable option for those seeking an affordable kneeling chair.

## Features

- Metal X-shaped base with four casters
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment for easy repositioning
- Thin but supportive padding on seat and knee pads
- Strong warranty coverage

## Pros

- Strong warranty
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment (easier than hand-screw models)
- Good portability
- Padding holds its position well despite being thin

## Cons

- Low-end build quality not built for long-term durability
- No range of motion while seated (no swivel, no rocking)
- Not comfortable for extended periods (reviewer wanted to switch after ~60 minutes)

## Specifications

- Base type: X-shaped metal frame with four casters
- Seat height adjustment: Pneumatic

## Pricing

Approximately $75.71 with free shipping.

## Overview

Surprisingly good for its low price, comparable to mid-range options like Hon Ignition.

## Features

- Materials: Mesh back and upholstered foam seat, plastic frame
- Adjustments: Height, lumbar height, tilt lock, recline resistance, headrest height
- Armrests: Flip up, not adjustable
- Ships assembled: No
- Weight limit: 300 pounds
- Warranty: 5 years

## Pricing

Well under $200.

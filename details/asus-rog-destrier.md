## Overview

The Asus ROG Destrier is a premium mesh gaming chair with an aggressive cyborg-inspired aesthetic. It features a full mesh seat and backrest with an advanced lumbar support system, designed specifically for long gaming sessions where breathability is a priority. It also offers an optional acoustic panel to reduce distracting background noise.

## Specifications

- **Upholstery:** High-tension mesh, PU foam
- **Maximum Weight:** 330 pounds
- **Backrest Width (Shoulder Level):** 14 inches
- **Seat Width (Point of Contact):** 22.4 inches

## Features

- High-tension mesh seat and backrest for breathability
- Adjustable lumbar support system
- 3D armrests with 360-degree rotation and height adjustment
- Headrest
- Recline to 135 degrees with four lockable positions (90, 105, 120, and 135 degrees)
- Tilt, tension, and depth adjustments
- Optional acoustic panel for noise reduction
- Cyborg-inspired all-black aggressive design

## Pros

- High quality materials used throughout
- Highly adjustable to dial in comfort
- Good, distinctive looks
- Mesh suspension perfect for heated gaming sessions

## Cons

- High price
- Wobbly armrests that don't lock, easily displaced by leaning

## Pricing

Positioned at a premium price point.

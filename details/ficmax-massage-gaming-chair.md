## Overview

Ficmax Massage Gaming Chair provides vibration massage for lower back relief, targeted at fatigue from prolonged gaming.

## Features

- USB-powered vibrating massage lumbar pillow
- High-back support with removable neck pillow
- 135° recline and tilt lock
- PU leather upholstery, easy to clean
- Class-4 gas lift for height adjustment

## Use Cases

- Long gaming marathons requiring recovery breaks
- Casual and entry-level gaming setups

## Specifications

- Weight capacity: 300 lbs
- Seat height: 18-22 inches

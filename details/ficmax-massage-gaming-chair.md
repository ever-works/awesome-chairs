## Overview

The Ficmax Massage Gaming Chair is a budget gaming chair that combines a massage lumbar pillow with traditional racing-chair design, offering comfort extras at an accessible price for casual gamers and first-time buyers.

## Features

- **USB Massage Lumbar**: Vibrating massage cushion for lower back comfort
- **Headrest Pillow**: Removable neck support
- **High-Back Design**: Full back coverage
- **Reclining Function**: Up to 135° tilt with lock mechanism
- **PU Leather**: Budget-friendly, easy-to-clean surface
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 300 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 135°
- Seat Height Range: 18" to 22"
- Warranty: 1-year limited warranty

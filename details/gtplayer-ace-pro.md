## Overview

Most comfortable cheap gaming chair tested, keeps legs from soreness longer than pricier options. Speakers provide decent sound, easy to pair with PC or phone.

## Features

- Built-in Bluetooth speakers in backrest
- Footrest (manual pull-out)
- Reclines to 150 degrees with lock
- Firm backrest and seat cushion
- One-year warranty

## Pricing

Most affordable recommendation, typically under $200.

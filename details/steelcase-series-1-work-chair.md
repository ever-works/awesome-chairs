## Steelcase Series 1 Work Chair

### Overview
The **Steelcase Series 1 Work Chair** is a budget-friendly ergonomic office chair designed to provide excellent support and comfort for a variety of body types, ideal for those seeking quality seating without a hefty price tag.

### Features
- **Adjustability**: Seat height (5" range), seat depth (2¼" range), height-adjustable lumbar (2¼" range)
- **4D Arms**: Height (5"), width (4"), depth (2" retract), pivot (40° inward/outward)
- **Comfort Dial**: Weight-activated, boosted, or upright back lock
- **3D Microknit Back**: Breathable, supportive backrest
- **Dual-wheel casters**: Carpet; optional soft casters for hard floors

### Specifications
- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- CarbonNeutral Product

### Pricing
Starting at approximately $500. See [Steelcase Series 1 Spec Guide](https://www.steelcase.com/content/uploads/2024/02/Series-1-Spec-Guide.pdf).

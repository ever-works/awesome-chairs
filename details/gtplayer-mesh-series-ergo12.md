## Overview

The GTPlayer Mesh Series Ergo12 is an affordable mesh office chair designed for extended sitting sessions. Its breathable mesh back and adjustable lumbar support make it a solid choice for budget-conscious first-time buyers and secondary desk setups.

## Features

- **Breathable Mesh Back**: Promotes airflow for all-day comfort
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable lower back positioning
- **Height-Adjustable Armrests**: Basic arm positioning
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift
- **Reclining Function**: Tilt mechanism with tension control
- **Swivel Rolling Base**: Five-star base with smooth casters

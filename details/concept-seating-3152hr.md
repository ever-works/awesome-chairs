## Overview

The Concept Seating 3152HR is a bestselling 24-hour rated office chair manufactured in Wisconsin, combining durability with extensive ergonomic adjustability. It features a unique seven-leg base for enhanced stability and movement.

## Key Specifications

- **Weight Capacity:** 550 lbs.
- **Seat Width:** 23" wide
- **Arms Width Adjustment:** 5" total (2.5" per side)
- **Starting Price:** ~$2,159.38

## Features

- Unique seven-leg base for easier movement and better stability than standard five-leg designs
- Seat height and seat depth adjustment
- Five-position tilt lock
- Adjustable height backrest
- Inflatable lumbar support
- Fully articulating headrest (can function as neck roll, headrest, or rotate away completely)
- Swing away T-arms option available
- Dymetrol suspension seat for long-period support
- Made in Wisconsin

## Best For

Users seeking a durable 24-hour rated chair with extensive ergonomic adjustments. Ideal for shift workers, control rooms, and environments where chairs see continuous use.

## Considerations

- The 23" wide seat pan, combined with the Dymetrol suspension, may feel narrower than advertised
- Aesthetics are utilitarian rather than sleek
- Users under 5'6" may benefit from the optional partial foot ring (additional $114.84) for proper foot support

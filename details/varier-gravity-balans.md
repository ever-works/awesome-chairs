## Overview

The Varier Gravity Balans is the ultimate ergonomic chair designed for both work and relaxation. It uniquely transitions between a kneeling chair, an upright sitting position, and a fully reclined position, meeting almost every seating requirement for exceptional ergonomics without strain.

## Seating Positions

- **Kneeling Position**: Lean forward to use it as a traditional kneeling chair, promoting active sitting and proper spine alignment
- **Upright Position**: Shift your weight slightly backwards with feet on the floor or shin cushions for variety
- **Reclined Position**: Gently push away from the ground to recline for relaxation and recovery

## Features

- Extremely comfortable with best-possible support when reclining
- Versatile multi-position design adapts to different activities
- Promotes active sitting and movement throughout the day
- Reduces strain through natural body positioning
- High-quality construction consistent with Varier standards

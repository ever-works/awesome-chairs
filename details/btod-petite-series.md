## Overview

The BTOD Petite Series is an affordable, highly customizable office chair designed for petite and small individuals. It is made in Taiwan and offers a wide selection of mechanisms, arms, and back options to suit individual preferences. An even smaller variant, the BTOD Bantam, is also available for those needing a more compact chair.

## Dimensions

- Minimum seat height: 15.5 inches
- Seat depth: 16 inches (adjustable with seat slider add-on)
- Arm height range: 5 to 7.5 inches (standard two-way arms)
- Arm width range: 16 to 20 inches

## Features

- Thick 4-inch padded seat for a soft, plush sitting experience
- Customizable options: different mechanisms, arms, back options
- Standard two-way arm setup with optional upgrades
- Seat slider add-on for adjustable seat depth
- Limited lifetime warranty: lifetime coverage on chair frame and parts, five years on foam and fabric

## BTOD Bantam Variant

An even smaller option within the Petite series. The Bantam features a smaller seat, smaller back, and a smaller seat height range compared to the BTOD Petite, designed for individuals who need a more compact chair.

## Pricing

Starting at $507.99 (2026). Price increases as additional adjustment options are added.

## Overview

The Blacklyte Athena Pro is a traditional racing-style gaming chair with a bolstered seat and high backrest. It features the familiar design language of major gaming chair brands but distinguishes itself with quality fabric upholstery and a comprehensive set of adjustments.

## Features

- Bolstered seat with racing-style design
- High backrest
- 4D armrests
- Built-in lumbar support adjustable with knobs on the sides
- Magnetic neck pillow
- 155-degree recline
- Fabric upholstery
- Maximum floor-to-seat height of 20.1 inches

## Pros

- Well-made construction
- Fabric upholstery comparable to or better than SecretLab SoftWeave
- Comprehensive adjustment options

## Cons

- Not particularly unique design
- Maximum floor-to-seat height (20.1 inches) is lower than most competitors (21-22 inches typical)
- May not be suitable for users who prefer a higher sitting position

## Pricing

Competitively priced in the racing-style gaming chair category.

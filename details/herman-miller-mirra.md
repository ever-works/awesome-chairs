## Overview

The Herman Miller Mirra is an ergonomic task chair designed by Studio 7.5. It features a flexible polymer back that responds to the user's movements while providing support.

## Features

- Flexible polymer backrest
- Adjustable lumbar support
- Tilt tension control with forward tilt
- Adjustable arms
- Pneumatic height adjustment
- Harmonic tilt mechanism

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: 350 lbs
- Seat Height: 16"–20.5"
- Warranty: 12-year Herman Miller warranty

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing, lower than the Aeron at comparable condition.

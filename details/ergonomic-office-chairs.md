## Summary of Herman Miller Ergonomic Office Chairs

Based on detailed testing across various Herman Miller ergonomic office chairs including the Embody, Aeron, and Vantum.

## Key Features Across Models

- **Adjustable armrests:** Available across models for personalized comfort
- **Seat height adjustment:** Standard for all models to match different desk setups
- **Recline settings:** Multiple models offer adjustable recline for different working positions
- **Lumbar support options:** Ranging from basic PostureFit to advanced PostureFit SL
- **Mesh options:** Available on select models for enhanced airflow

## Models Tested

- **Embody**: Premium model with Pixelated support system and advanced back fit adjustment
- **Aeron**: Classic ergonomic with PostureFit SL and adjustable arms
- **Vantum**: Entry-level model with integrated PostureFit (no separate lumbar adjustment)

## Notable Findings

- The Vantum shares the same base model as the Aeron but lacks separate lumbar adjustment, using integrated PostureFit instead
- The Aeron with PostureFit SL provides the most adjustable lumbar support
- The Embody offers the most advanced back support with its Pixelated system

## Pricing

Pricing range is approximately $930 (Vantum) to $2,045 (Embody Gaming).

## Overview

Provides good value with limited but effective adjustments, suited for larger frames.

## Features

- Materials: Mesh, plastic frame, upholstered foam seat
- Adjustments: Height, seat depth, tilt resistance, forward tilt
- Armrests: Height and width adjustable
- Ships assembled: No
- Weight limit: 300 pounds
- Warranty: Lifetime (some parts 12 years)

## Pricing

Below $500.

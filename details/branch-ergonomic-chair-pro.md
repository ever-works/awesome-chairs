## Overview

The Branch Ergonomic Chair Pro is Branch's most advanced model with 14 points of adjustment, designed to be the most comfortable office chair for professionals who demand precision ergonomics and comprehensive customization.

## Features

- **14 Points of Adjustment**: Industry-leading customization options
- **Advanced Lumbar Support**: Adjustable height and depth lumbar system
- **Premium Mesh**: High-grade breathable mesh for optimal comfort
- **4D Armrests**: Fully adjustable in height, width, depth, and angle
- **Adjustable Headrest**: Removable and fully adjustable for neck support
- **Seat Depth Adjustment**: Customize seat depth for proper leg support
- **Tilt Tension Control**: Fine-tune recline resistance

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 275 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 130°
- Warranty: Lifetime warranty on frame, 5 years on parts
- Height Range: Suitable for users 5'0" to 6'4"
- Certifications: GREENGUARD Gold certified

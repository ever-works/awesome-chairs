## Headrest Review

The Clatina Mellet headrest is a ~$40 add-on that punches well above its price point in terms of quality and comfort.

## Headrest Features

- **Good value**: Approximately $40 for the headrest, offering strong performance for the price
- **Solid build quality**: Feels well-built with substantial weight
- **Good cushioning**: Comfortable padding without hard edges
- **Decent range of adjustability**: Easy-to-use adjustments, surprising for a chair at this price
- **Versatile use**: Can be used for tasking or moved out of the way
- **Stays in position**: Holds adjustments without unwanted movement or snapping into different positions
- **Consistent with chair quality**: Some reviewers noted the headrest may be better than the chair itself

## Expert Ratings

- Consensus: **B-Tier** (some reviewers rated it A-Tier)
- Described as one of the more comfortable headrests used across all price ranges
- Notable for offering A-tier comfort at a budget price point

## Overview

Core EX series provides premium endurance ergonomics at accessible pricing for gamers.

## Key Features

- 180° full recline with footrest
- 4D armrests (height, depth, angle, width)
- Adjustable lumbar pillow
- High-density cold-cure foam
- Wide seat base for 300 lbs capacity

## Use Cases

- Esports training: Precise arm positioning
- Streaming setups: Lounge recline for off-camera rest
- Endurance coding/gaming: All-day support

## Comparisons

- Vs Secretlab: Wider seat and deeper recline at lower cost
- Vs Noblechairs: Better adjustability for larger frames

## Pricing

$300-400, excellent value in premium budget segment.

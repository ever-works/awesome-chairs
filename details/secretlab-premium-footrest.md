## Overview

Secretlab's Premium Footrest uses PlushCell memory foam for a soft, cushy surface to rest feet on while seated at a desk. It has been noted for staying remarkably clean over extended use, even in environments with pets.

## Features

- PlushCell memory foam construction for soft comfort
- Quilted fabric cover that resists dirt and pet hair
- Patterned silicone base that keeps the footrest in place
- Cover is hand-washable for easy cleaning
- Available for $89

## Considerations

- Ideal use is when feet are not planted flat on the ground; designed for occasional propping rather than permanent foot rest

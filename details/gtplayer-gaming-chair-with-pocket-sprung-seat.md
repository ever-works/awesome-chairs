## Overview

The GTPLAYER Gaming Chair (under ~£150) features a quilted PU cover with a slim pocket-sprung layer above a high-density foam base — an unusual comfort feature for its price segment. Its black-and-white 'cow spot' inspired design and PVC finish make it both stylish and wear-resistant.

## Features

- **Seat Construction**: Quilted PU cover with breathable fabric, pocket-sprung layer, and high-density foam base
- **Material**: PVC finish (extremely resistant to wear and tear instead of standard PU leather), metal frame, foam padding
- **Lumbar Support**: Removable lumbar cushion
- **Headrest**: Removable headrest pillow
- **Armrests**: Fixed armrests (linked to backrest, move when reclining)
- **Recline**: 90° to 145° tilt with rocking function
- **Footrest**: Retractable slide-out footrest
- **Seat Height**: Adjustable, height range 125–135 cm overall

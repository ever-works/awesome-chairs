## Overview

The Comhoma CH511 is a mid-range gaming chair designed to balance affordability with comfort. It offers key features like lumbar support and adjustable armrests at a price point accessible to budget-conscious gamers and casual users setting up secondary workspaces.

## Features

- **Ergonomic Design**: Contoured backrest and seat
- **Lumbar Support**: Adjustable lower back cushion
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Height and width customization
- **Reclining Function**: Multiple positions for relaxation
- **PU Leather**: Durable, easy-to-clean surface
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

Starting at approximately $249.99

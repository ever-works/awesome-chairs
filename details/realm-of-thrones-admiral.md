## Headrest Review

The Realm of Thrones Admiral is a random Amazon purchase chair that features an unusually large mesh headrest.

## Headrest Features

- **Massive size**: Oversized headrest provides generous coverage
- **Height and pivot adjustment**: Offers both vertical and angular adjustment
- **Comfortable mesh**: More comfortable than the ErgoChair Pro with no hard points
- **Dual-mode support**: When sitting upright, it stays out of the way; when reclining, it engages comfortably
- **No uncomfortable frame contact**: Despite appearances of hard points, nothing is felt during actual use

## Expert Ratings

- Consensus: **C+ to B-Tier**
- More comfortable than similarly sized headrests such as on the Humanscale Freedom chair
- Better than the LE9 and ErgoChair Pro headrests in comfort

## Drawbacks

- Tendency to move when not desired
- Some reviewers feel it doesn't quite match the quality of the chair itself

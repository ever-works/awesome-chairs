## Overview

The ErgoChair Pro is Autonomous's mid-range ergonomic office chair featuring a mesh backrest with an adjustable headrest.

## Headrest Assessment

- **Tier Rank:** C+ (average across reviewers)
- **Adjustment:** Height and pivot adjustment with decent range
- **Comfort Issues:** The mesh material can feel abrasive on the neck; some users report it pulls hair
- **Positive Aspects:** Generous size, firm but comfortable fit, allows natural head movement

## Features

- Mesh backrest construction
- Adjustable headrest with height and pivot adjustment
- 3D armrests
- Seat depth slider
- Lumbar cushion with dual tension settings

## Pricing

Retail price: $499. 30-day return window, 2-year warranty.

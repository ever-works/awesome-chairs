## Overview

The Sihoo Doro C300 Pro is a top-tier option for those who need a chair with more advanced features, especially for long office hours. The C300 Pro is designed to accommodate users up to 6'3" and offers professional-grade ergonomic features.

## Features

- **Enhanced Lumbar System**: Professional-grade adjustable lumbar support
- **Extended Height Range**: Optimized for taller users up to 6'3"
- **4D Pro Armrests**: Premium adjustable armrests with enhanced range
- **Advanced Mesh Technology**: Superior breathability and support
- **Heavy-Duty Base**: Reinforced construction for enhanced stability
- **Premium Headrest**: Multi-directional adjustable headrest

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 330 lbs
- Height Range: Suitable for users 5'5" to 6'3"
- Recline Range: 90° to 135°
- Warranty: 3-year manufacturer warranty

## Overview

The Lino is Herman Miller's most affordable ergonomic office chair with all the quality you'd expect from the brand.

## Features

- **Minimal design:** Small backrest and seat for a minimal look
- **Adjustable arms:** Height and angle adjustable, with flip-up option
- **Adjustable seat:** Height adjustable via pneumatic lift
- **Built-in lumbar support:** Integrated into the suspension backrest
- **Breathable suspension back:** Similar to higher-end Herman Miller models
- **Optional accessories:** Add-on headrest and adjustable arms available

## Warranty

Comes with Herman Miller's standard 12-year warranty.

## Pricing

Starting at approximately $640 new (base configuration)

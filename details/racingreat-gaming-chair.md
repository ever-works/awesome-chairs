## Overview

The Racingreat Gaming Chair is an affordable racing-style gaming chair priced around $69.99. Despite its low cost, it delivers useful features typically found in more expensive models, making it a solid entry-level option for budget-conscious gamers.

## Features

- **Material**: Two-tone PU leather upholstery with one-piece metal frame and high-density moulded foam cushioning
- **Lumbar Support**: Removable lumbar cushion with strap clips
- **Headrest**: Removable headrest pillow with clip straps
- **Armrests**: Fixed padded armrests
- **Recline**: 90° to 155° tilt angle with tilt and lock mechanism
- **Footrest**: Slide-and-fold extendable footrest
- **Casters**: Low-noise nylon casters

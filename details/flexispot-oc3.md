## Overview

Affordable mesh hybrid chair for daily use.

## Features

- Breathable mesh backrest
- Adjustable 3D/4D armrests
- Lumbar support dial
- Cold mold foam seat
- 135° recline lock
- Aluminum wheelbase

## Use Cases

Great for entry-level gamers or remote workers on tight budgets needing all-day ventilation. Suitable for 6-8 hour office tasks with easy adjustments. Versatile for small spaces with compact footprint.

## Comparisons

Vs Sihoo M18: Better armrest range and mesh cooling at similar price; less padded seat.

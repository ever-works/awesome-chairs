## Overview

The Staples Hyken is a budget-friendly ergonomic office chair with a breathable mesh back and seat, designed for long hours at a desk in home or office settings. It includes integrated lumbar support and a headrest, and can recline for more relaxed sitting.

## Features

- **Breathable Mesh Back**: Ventilated design for airflow
- **Breathable Mesh Seat**: No heat buildup in the seat
- **Integrated Lumbar Support**: Built-in lower back contour
- **Headrest**: Adjustable neck support
- **Tilt with Lock**: Reclining functionality
- **Adjustable Height**: Pneumatic gas lift

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 250 lbs
- Recline: Basic tilt with lock
- Warranty: Limited manufacturer warranty

## Pricing

Starting at approximately $149.99, frequently discounted to around $140.

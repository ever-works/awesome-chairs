## Overview

The Odinlake Ergo Pro 630 is a budget-friendly ergonomic chair under $300 that offers comprehensive adjustability and comfort features typically found in more expensive chairs, making premium ergonomics accessible to more users.

## Features

- **Comprehensive Adjustability**: Multiple adjustment points
- **Lumbar Support**: Adjustable lower back support system
- **Breathable Mesh**: Promotes airflow for comfort
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Multi-directional arm positioning
- **Tilt Mechanism**: Smooth recline with tension control
- **Quality Construction**: Durable build at budget price

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 300 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 125°
- Seat Height Range: 18\" to 21.5\"
- Warranty: 2-year manufacturer warranty
- Certifications: BIFMA compliant

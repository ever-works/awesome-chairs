## Steelcase Gesture Chair

**Brand:** Steelcase  
**Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  

### Features
- **3D LiveBack® Technology:** Mimics the natural motion of the spine, providing continuous support and encouraging movement.
- **360-Degree Arms:** Offers the largest width range on the market, allowing for a customizable fit that accommodates various postures and preferences.
- **Contoured Backrest:** Supports the natural s-shape of the spine, promoting healthy posture throughout the day.
- **Deep Recline Capability:** Allows users to recline deeply, supporting various working postures and ensuring comfort over prolonged periods.
- **Intuitive Adjustments:** Simple, user-friendly controls for seat height, seat depth, and recline tension.

### Recognition
- **Best Office Chair:** Selected by the New York Times Wirecutter as "Our Pick for Best Office Chair" from 2015–2024.

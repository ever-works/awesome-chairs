## Overview

The Eames Executive Chair is an iconic mid-century modern chair designed by Charles and Ray Eames, originally created in 1958 for the owners of the Herman Miller company.

## Features

- Premium full-grain leather upholstery
- Aluminum alloy die-cast frame with polished finish
- 20° tilt mechanism for natural reclining
- Ergonomic padding and cushioning
- Available with polished or chrome frame finishes
- Multiple leather color options

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- Tilt Range: 20°
- Overall Dimensions: 26.75\"W × 32.75\"D × 40\"H
- Seat Height: 16.5\"–19.5\"
- Warranty: 12-year Herman Miller warranty

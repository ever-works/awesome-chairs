## Overview

The Herman Miller Sayl is an ergonomic task chair designed by Yves Béhar, featuring a distinctive elastomeric suspension frame that flexes to provide responsive support.

## Features

- Adjustable lumbar support
- Adjustable arms (height and angle)
- Tilt limiter with forward seat angle
- Adjustable tension control
- Compact frame suitable for smaller users
- Sustainable design and assembly

## Warranty

Comes with Herman Miller's standard 12-year warranty.

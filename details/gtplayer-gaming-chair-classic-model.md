## Overview

The GTPLAYER Gaming Chair Classic Model GT099 is an affordable gaming chair tailored for casual gamers and first-time buyers who need reliable seating without premium pricing. With its racing car seat-inspired design, this chair provides basic ergonomic support and gaming-style aesthetics.

## Features

- **Classic Racing Design**: Sporty aesthetic for gaming setups
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Smooth, easy-to-clean surface
- **Height-Adjustable Armrests**: Customizable arm positioning
- **Reclining Function**: Up to 155° tilt with lock mechanism
- **Removable Cushions**: Lumbar and neck pillow support
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

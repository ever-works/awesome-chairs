## Overview

The BetterPosture Jazzy by Jobri is one of the most unique kneeling chairs on the market, featuring a 5-star base with casters similar to a standard office chair. It is the most mobile kneeling chair available.

## Features

- 5-star base with good quality casters for excellent mobility
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment (taller range than most kneeling chairs)
- Adjustable backrest connected via J-bar
- Large seat with high-density foam padding
- Swivel function

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: 250 lbs

Approximately $294.99 with free shipping.

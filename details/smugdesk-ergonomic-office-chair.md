## Overview

The SmugDesk Ergonomic Office Chair is an affordable mesh office chair offering essential ergonomic features for home office users on a tight budget. This chair provides adjustable lumbar support and breathable design for comfortable daily use.

## Features

- **Breathable Mesh**: Promotes airflow to prevent heat buildup
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable lower back support
- **Height Adjustment**: Pneumatic gas lift
- **Flip-Up Armrests**: Space-saving arm design
- **Tilt Function**: Basic recline mechanism
- **Easy Assembly**: Simple setup process

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 250 lbs
- Recline Range: Basic tilt function
- Seat Height Range: 17" to 21"
- Warranty: 1-year manufacturer warranty

## Overview

The Vipp 453 Swivel Chair is an extra-padded version of Vipp's original swiveling chair, combining polished aluminum construction with aniline leather upholstery. Available with casters or floor gliders and offering full 360-degree rotation, it's designed for those who prioritize style alongside seating function.

## Specifications

- **Dimensions:** 26.4"L x 26.4"W x 30.5"H
- **Materials:** Polished aluminum, aniline leather
- **Warranty:** 5 years
- **Adjustability:** Height adjustable
- **Options:** Available with casters or floor gliders

## Features

- Polished aluminum frame with cut-out design details
- Premium aniline leather upholstery
- 360-degree swivel rotation
- Choice of casters or floor gliders
- Extra-padding compared to Vipp's original swivel chair
- Encourages upright sitting posture

## Pros

- Stylish leather and metal design suitable for non-corporate home offices
- Smooth 360-degree spin
- Versatile base options (casters or floor gliders)
- Sleek aesthetic that makes you look "chic by extension"
- Functions as posture corrective by encouraging straight sitting

## Cons

- Not as comfortable or adjustable as dedicated ergonomic chairs
- Back may be too low for taller people
- Cut-out design lacks the support of fully enclosed ergonomic seats
- May cause lower back cramping after extended sitting periods
- Better suited for shorter individuals
- Lacks adjustable armrests, lumbar support, and other ergonomic features

## Overview

The BestOffice High-Back Ergonomic Chair is a budget-friendly seating solution featuring a high backrest, mesh construction, and lumbar support. It is suitable for both home and office environments, providing comfort and support for extended periods of sitting.

## Features

- **High Back**: Offers ample support for the upper back and shoulders
- **Mesh Construction**: Breathable material for improved airflow
- **Lumbar Support**: Designed to support the lower back and promote better posture
- **Adjustable Seat Height**: Pneumatic gas lift for customization
- **Swivel Rolling Base**: Provides mobility and ease of movement around the workspace
- **Comfortable Padded Seat**: Cushioned seating for extended comfort

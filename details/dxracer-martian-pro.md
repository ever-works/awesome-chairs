## Overview

The DXRacer Martian Pro is a high-end gaming chair with advanced tech features for superior comfort during extended sessions.

## Features

- Electric adjustments replacing manual levers
- Built-in airbag lumbar support
- Seat heating and cooling
- Integrated massager
- Magnetic headrest pillow that stays in place
- Adjustable armrests for various body sizes
- Firm seat cushion that retains shape
- Built-in battery lasting weeks with occasional feature use; full charge in hours
- Solid premium build quality; assembly in ~20 minutes

## Pricing

Approximately £580 in the UK (sold out at time of review).

## Use Cases

Ideal for gamers and professionals enduring 10-12 hour sessions, providing relief for back issues and climate control for comfort.

## Overview

The Autofull Bunny Chair is a themed gaming chair designed for users who want a standout aesthetic piece. It has gone viral on social media for its distinctive bunny-ear headrest cover and bunny tail back cushion.

## Features

- Bunny ear headrest cover and bunny tail back accessory
- Removable and adjustable lumbar cushion and headrest
- Adjustable armrests with soft white covers
- Comfortable for long sitting sessions
- Available in multiple colorways centered around pink/white aesthetic

## Dimensions

- Seat depth: 20"
- Seat width: 15" (without bolsters)
- Height: 51" (without bunny ears)
- Max weight capacity: 330 lbs

## Pricing

Typically priced around $400, but often available on sale.

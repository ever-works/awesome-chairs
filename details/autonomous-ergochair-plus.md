## Overview

The Autonomous ErgoChair Plus is an ergonomic office chair built with a unique frameless design using thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) material that contours to the user's body. Priced at $399, it focuses on comfort and durability over aesthetics.

## Specifications

| Specification | Value |
|---|---|
| Price | $399 |
| Weight Capacity | 300 lbs |
| Seat Height | 17–21 in (43.2–53.3 cm) |
| Adjustable Arms | 7–11 in (17.8–27.9 cm) |
| Tilt Range | 25 degrees |
| Overall Dimensions | 28 × 28 × 40–44 in |

## Adjustable Features

- Armrest height adjustment
- Back tilt angle (up to 25 degrees)
- Back tilt tension control
- Seat height adjustment (17–21 inches)

## Key Features

- **Frameless TPE construction** — Thermoplastic elastomer material is highly malleable and contours to the user's back without a rigid frame, providing adaptive spinal support
- **Spring-based seat cushioning** — Uses springs instead of foam to push back against the user, preventing dips and maintaining even pressure distribution over time
- **Enhanced airflow** — The perforated back design and TPE material allow air to move freely, keeping the user cool during extended sitting sessions
- **Even weight distribution** — The combination of TPE and springs distributes weight evenly across the seat and back, reducing lower back and tailbone pain
- **Durable build** — Resistant to sagging and degradation that commonly affects foam cushions and leather chairs over time

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Increases airflow and keeps users cool
- Even weight distribution reduces pressure points
- Very durable construction that resists sagging
- Conforms well to different body types
- Supports up to 300 lbs

**Cons:**
- Plain, utilitarian design that lacks visual appeal
- Limited to four basic adjustments
- No headrest included
- No seat tilt adjustment
- Slightly expensive compared to chairs with similar features

## Overview

The Nightingale CXO is a heavy-duty ergonomic office chair manufactured in Canada. It is the heaviest and most solid mesh chair in its class, with a weight capacity of 350 lbs. The chair combines mesh back support with ENERSORB memory foam on the seat and headrest.

## Features

- ENERSORB memory foam headrest that is height adjustable with tilt functionality
- Built-in coat hanger on the back of the headrest
- ENERSORB memory foam seat cushion for a soft, sink-in feel
- Adjustable lumbar support
- 4-way adjustable arms
- 350 lbs weight capacity
- Made in Canada
- Fully loaded with ergonomic adjustments

## Sizing

The CXO with headrest does not accommodate users shorter than 5'3", as the headrest would push the top of the head forward rather than cradle the neck.

## Headrest Review

The Engineered Now H4 is an aftermarket headrest designed for the Herman Miller Aeron chair, compatible with both Classic and Remastered versions.

## Headrest Features

- **Dual compatibility**: Works with both Classic and Remastered Aeron models
- **Two pivot points**: Provides a good range of adjustability
- **Quality mesh material**: Feels solid and well-made
- **Large headrest surface**: Generous coverage area
- **H4 version**: Designed for taller users; the H3 version (shorter people) has a smaller headrest with a huge U-curve that is not recommended
- **Good aesthetics**: Looks good on the Aeron and matches well

## Expert Ratings

- Consensus: **B-Tier** (some placed it at low A-Tier)
- Minor drawbacks include slight frame feel at the outer edges and some self-sliding during movement
- Recommended for tall users due to its high range of vertical adjustment

## Pricing

Mid-range aftermarket accessory; less expensive than the Atlas option

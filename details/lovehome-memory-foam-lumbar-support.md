## Overview

The LoveHome Memory Foam Lumbar Support is an affordable pillow-style lumbar support that helps keep the back straight and supported. It is suitable for use in office chairs, cars, or wheelchairs.

## Features

- Memory foam construction for comfortable back support
- Helps promote proper posture by keeping the back straight
- Washable cover
- Two adjustable straps plus an extension strap to secure the pillow to the seat
- Versatile: usable in office chairs, cars, and wheelchairs

## Pricing

Priced at approximately $22, making it an affordable option to try before investing in a new chair with built-in lumbar support.

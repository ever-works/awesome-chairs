## Overview

The La-Z-Boy Westley Big and Tall Executive Chair is a premium big and tall office chair from a trusted name in comfort, featuring advanced ergonomic and AIR Health Technology innovations.

## Features

- **Layered-Body Cushioning Technology:** Multi-layer foam for lasting comfort
- **Memory-Foam Lumbar Pillow:** Adjustable height for personalized lower back support
- **AIR Health Technology:** Integrated ergonomic lumbar support system
- **Height-Adjustable Armrests:** Padded arms for shoulder relief
- **Waterfall Seat Edge:** Reduces leg and hip pressure
- **Bonded Leather Upholstery:** Durable and professional appearance

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 400 lbs
- Seat Width: Oversized for big and tall users
- Recline: Multi-position tilt with lock
- Warranty: 5-year limited warranty

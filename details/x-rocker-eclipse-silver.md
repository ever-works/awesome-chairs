## Overview

The X Rocker Eclipse Silver is a floor gaming chair designed for relaxed, console-focused setups where comfort and space-saving design matter most. It offers a comfortable floor-based seating solution with built-in audio compatibility for an immersive gaming experience.

## Specifications

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Weight Capacity | Up to 300 lbs |
| Chair Dimensions | 31" Depth, 27.5" Width, 16.5" Height |
| Materials | Faux Leather, Breathable Mesh, High-Density Foam |
| Frame & Base | Alloy Steel Frame, Hard Plastic Base |
| Backrest Reclinability | N/A (Rocking Design) |
| Special Features | Foldable Design, Built-in Audio, Wireless Connectivity |

## Features

- Curved ergonomic design for natural back support
- Built-in audio compatibility that works with gaming consoles
- Wireless connectivity for hassle-free audio integration
- Padded seat and backrest with firm, comfortable high-density foam
- Durable and attractive faux leather (vinyl) exterior
- Foldable design for easy storage and transport
- Space-saving form factor, ideal for bedrooms or dorms
- Solid curved shape that supports your back naturally
- Holds up well over time even with repeated use

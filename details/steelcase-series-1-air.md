### Steelcase Series 1 Air

**Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  
**Brand:** Steelcase  
**Website:** https://store.steelcase.com/seating/ergonomic-chairs/steelcase-series-1-air

#### Overview
Steelcase Series 1 Air is a breathable mesh-back variant of the Steelcase Series 1 ergonomic chair, designed for compact workspaces and all-day computer use.

#### Features
- **3D Microknit mesh back** for increased breathability
- **Adjustable lumbar support**: Height-adjustable lower-back alignment
- **4D armrests**: Height, width, depth, and pivot (40°)
- **Comfort Dial**: Weight-activated, boosted, or upright back lock
- **Compact form factor** suitable for smaller or space-constrained workspaces
- **Dual-wheel casters**: Carpet; optional soft casters for hard floors

#### Specifications
- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- CarbonNeutral Product

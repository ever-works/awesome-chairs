## Overview

The Yonisee Office Chair is a budget mesh office chair available at an accessible price point, offering basic ergonomic features for cost-conscious first-time buyers and secondary desk use.

## Features

- **Mesh Back Construction**: Breathable ventilated back panel
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift
- **Basic Armrests**: Fixed or limited-adjustment arms
- **Tilt Mechanism**: Basic recline functionality
- **Swivel Rolling Base**: Five-star base with casters

## Pricing

Starting at approximately $149.99

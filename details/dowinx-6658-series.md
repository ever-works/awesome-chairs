## Overview

The Dowinx 6658 Series is a gaming chair specifically designed for smaller or undersized gamers who find most chairs too large or wide. With adjustable dimensions and ergonomic features, it provides a comfortable, customized sitting experience.

## Specifications

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Weight Capacity | Up to 290 lbs |
| Chair Dimensions | 25.2" Depth, 21.3" Width, 51.2" Height |
| Materials | Breathable Cloth Fabric, Pocket Springs, Shaped Foam |
| Frame & Base | Sturdy Metal Frame, Heavy-Duty Metal Base |
| Backrest Reclinability | 90° to 135° |
| Special Features | Ergonomic Design, USB-Powered Massage Lumbar Support, Retractable Footrest |

## Features

- Adjustable armrests and seat height for a custom fit on smaller frames
- Ergonomic design with adjustable lumbar pillow that helps reduce back pain
- USB-powered massage lumbar support
- Cushioned seat designed for both comfort and durability
- Tilt tension and lockable recline for versatile seating
- Retractable footrest
- Pocket spring technology for enhanced seat comfort
- Breathable cloth fabric upholstery
- Durable construction built for extended use and longevity

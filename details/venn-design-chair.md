## Overview

Venn Design has elevated the basic ball chair concept into a premium active and ergonomic seating solution. The chair uses a unique multi-layered laminate construction that provides proper sitting load distribution and temperature control for better comfort. The design is versatile enough for desk tasking as well as stretching, yoga, and exercise.

## Features

- Premium multi-layered laminate construction
- Proper sitting load distribution across the seat surface
- Temperature control for better long-term comfort
- Recognized as true active and ergonomic seating
- Promotes movement and engages core muscles
- Versatile: can be used for desk tasking, stretching, yoga, and exercise
- Multi-use and fun design aesthetic

## Use Cases

- Home office workers seeking an alternative to traditional chairs
- Users who want a seating solution that doubles as exercise equipment
- Those looking for a creative, engaging way to reduce sedentary time

## Important Considerations

- May not work for everyone's all-day sitting needs
- The ball chair design requires some adaptation period
- Not ideal for environments requiring prolonged static postures

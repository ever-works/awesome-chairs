## Headrest Review

The Neutral Posture 8000 headrest is consistently ranked at the very top of headrest evaluations.

## Headrest Features

- **Extreme range of adjustability**: Can flip out of the way entirely if not needed
- **Versatile positioning**: Can act as a neck roll or support the full back of the head
- **Comfortable padding**: Described as super poofy and well-padded
- **Stays in position**: Holds its place securely without unwanted movement
- **Dual-mode use**: Can be used in relaxed mode aligned with the backrest when reclining (ideal for watching movies, phone calls), or articulated forward for task mode
- **Large contour**: Big and spacious design
- **Aesthetic quality**: Matches the chair's design language and build quality

## Expert Ratings

- Consensus: **S-Tier** (highest rating)
- Reviewers noted they could not identify any changes needed to improve the headrest

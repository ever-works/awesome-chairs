## Overview

The DXRacer Martian Pro Red Triangle is a premium gaming chair distinguished by its built-in heating and cooling system, plus a built-in massage feature. It combines classic racing style aesthetics with advanced comfort technology for users who experience temperature regulation issues during long gaming sessions.

## Specifications

- **Upholstery:** EPU Leatherette
- **Maximum Weight:** Under 275 pounds (L and XL)
- **Backrest Length:** 31.9 inches (L) / 33.3 inches (XL)
- **Backrest Width (Shoulder Level):** 21.3 inches (L) / 22.8 inches (XL)
- **Seat Width (Point of Contact):** 22 inches (L) / 23.4 inches (XL)

## Features

- Solid steel frame construction
- Built-in heating and cooling system with 10-minute timer limits for each mode
- Built-in massage feature
- 4D lumbar support with two supports adjustable in four directions
- 4D armrests
- Motorized recline to 135 degrees
- Magnetic headrest
- Battery pack with up to 360 hours of runtime per full charge
- Less pronounced bucket seat design for improved comfort during long sessions

## Pros

- Solid steel frame construction
- Built-in massager works effectively
- Highly adjustable with 4D armrests, lumbar support, and magnetic headrest

## Cons

- Expensive
- Heavy (over 70 pounds)
- Seat warmer and vent buttons are placed too close to the sitting area
- Motorized recline can be frustrating for quick adjustments
- No battery charge indicator

## Pricing

Priced at $900, making it one of the more expensive gaming chairs available.

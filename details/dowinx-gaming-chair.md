## Overview

The Dowinx Gaming Chair is positioned at the more affordable end of the gaming chair market while offering features typically found in higher-priced models, including a slide-out footrest and a massage pillow.

## Features

- Retractable slide-out footrest for relaxing between gaming sessions
- Tilt range of 90° to 135° for varied seating and reclining positions
- Massage pillow for lower back comfort
- 4D adjustable armrests for customized arm support
- PU leather upholstery with high-density foam cushioning
- Iron frame construction for durability
- Height adjustment via pneumatic gas lift
- Maximum load capacity of 200 kg

## Pricing

Budget-friendly pricing, positioned at the affordable end of the gaming chair market.

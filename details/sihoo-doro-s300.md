The Sihoo Doro S300 is one of Sihoo's most advanced ergonomic chairs, designed with superior back support and featuring an innovative anti-gravity mechanism that reduces pressure on your spine. This premium chair offers an exceptional combination of comfort and support for professionals who spend long hours seated.

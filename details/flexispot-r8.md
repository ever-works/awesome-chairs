## Overview

The R8 provides a relaxed approach to ergonomic support, prioritizing comfort through natural movement and recline over rigid lumbar reinforcement.

## Features

- Basic lumbar support
- Design encourages frequent position changes to reduce pressure buildup
- Supports relaxed sitting styles with easy reclining
- Suitable for alternating between tasks

## Best For

- Executives
- Lighter, less desk-bound use
- Frequent reclining and posture shifts

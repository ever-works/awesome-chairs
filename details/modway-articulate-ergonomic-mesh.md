## Overview

The Modway Articulate Ergonomic Mesh is a budget-friendly office chair designed to offer comfort and breathability with a mesh back and padded seat. It is an excellent choice for cost-conscious users seeking a lightweight, adjustable office chair.

## Features

- **Breathable Mesh Back**: Designed with a ventilated mesh panel for air circulation and reduced heat buildup
- **Padded Seat**: Soft cushion for seated comfort
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Height-adjustable padded arms
- **Height Adjustment**: Pneumatic gas lift
- **Swivel Base**: Five-star base with smooth-rolling casters
- **Multiple Colors**: Available in a variety of color options

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 200 lbs
- Dimensions: 26"D x 26"W x 41.5"H
- Warranty: 1-year limited warranty

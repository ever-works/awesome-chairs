## Overview

The Sihoo Doro C300 is designed for maximum comfort, breathability, and adaptability. It features a self-adaptive lumbar support system that automatically adjusts to your spine's curves, reducing strain during long work sessions. The mesh backrest ensures superior airflow, keeping you cool even during extended periods of sitting.

## Features

- **Self-Adaptive Lumbar Support**: Provides dynamic lumbar support adjusting as you move
- **4D Coordinated Armrests**: Fully adjustable to cater to all sitting postures
- **Smart Weight-Sensing Chassis**: Automatically adjusts based on your body weight
- **Breathable Mesh**: Premium mesh material for optimal airflow
- **Synchro-Tilt Mechanism**: Synchronized seat and backrest movement
- **Adjustable Headrest**: Customizable head and neck support

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 300 lbs
- Height Range: Designed for users up to 6'3"
- Recline Range: Up to 126 degrees
- Warranty: 3-year manufacturer warranty

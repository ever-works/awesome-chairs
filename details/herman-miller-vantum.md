## Overview

The Herman Miller Vantum is Herman Miller's first dedicated gaming chair, designed in collaboration with Logitech G. It combines the comfort of a gaming chair with the support and ergonomics of a premium office chair, making it ideal for those who both game and work from home.

## Features

- Knit mesh backrest and headrest for breathability during long sessions
- Fabric seat with comfortable foam cushion and waterfall edge to prevent pressure buildup
- Adjustable dedicated lumbar support mechanism
- Thoracic pads in the backrest to support upper back and shoulders, not just the lower back
- Aluminum base for durability
- Multiple two-tone color combinations available
- 117-degree recline angle
- Supports up to 350 pounds
- Seat height range: 40.25 - 44.25 inches
- Dimensions: 30.5 x 29 x 44.25 inches (W x D x H)

## Pricing

The Herman Miller Vantum is priced at $930. It is positioned as a premium investment piece for serious gamers who also work from home.

## Warranty

Comes with a 12-year warranty, reflecting Herman Miller's confidence in the chair's long-term durability.

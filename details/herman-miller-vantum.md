## Overview

The Herman Miller Vantum gaming chair, developed with Logitech G, blends office ergonomics with gaming design for hybrid use. Features performance knit back and advanced tilt for prolonged comfort.

## Features

- Knit mesh backrest and headrest for breathability
- Adjustable lumbar and thoracic support pads
- Fabric seat with waterfall edge for circulation
- Aluminum base, 117-degree recline, 350 lb capacity
- More gaming-focused adjustments vs standard Aeron

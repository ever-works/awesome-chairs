## Overview

Razer Iskur V2 is engineered for VR/AR smart integration, supporting haptic devices for tactile feedback during gameplay.

## Features

- Haptic feedback compatible for synchronized VR vibrations
- 180-degree recline with anti-gravity positioning for relaxed extended sessions
- 6D adaptive lumbar support auto-adjusts to spine
- RGB lighting syncs with gaming setups
- Breathable mesh and memory foam for long wear

## Use Cases

- Extended VR/AR sessions (4+ hours) with full-body immersion
- Competitive esports with haptic enhancements
- Home sim racing or flight sims requiring recline

## Comparisons

- Superior haptic integration vs. DXRacer Tank (no haptics)
- Better auto-lumbar than Secretlab Titan (manual adjust)

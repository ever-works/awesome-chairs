## Overview

The CushionLab Seat Cushion is a memory foam cushion that can be added to an existing chair to improve comfort during long sitting sessions. It is particularly useful for those who cannot upgrade their seat right away.

## Features

- Memory foam construction for comfortable all-day sitting
- Odor-resistant materials
- Washable cover
- Adds a noticeable amount of height to the seat

## Considerations

- Adding height may make typing on a keyboard awkward if the chair is not adjustable
- Listed price of $85 is rarely the actual selling price; the cushion is frequently available at a lower price

## Pricing

Listed at $85, but commonly sold at a discounted price.

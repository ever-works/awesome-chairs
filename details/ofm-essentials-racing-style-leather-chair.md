## Overview

The OFM Essentials Racing Style Chair is a budget-friendly gaming chair offering racing-style design with bonded leather upholstery and segmented padding at an accessible price point.

## Features

- **High-Back Racing Design**: Sporty gaming aesthetic
- **Bonded Leather Upholstery**: Easy-to-clean professional appearance
- **Segmented Padding**: Contoured comfort zones
- **Padded Armrests**: Fixed arm support
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift
- **Multiple Colors**: Available in various color options

## Overview

The GTPLAYER Pro Series GT002 is a budget-friendly gaming chair offering premium features like 4D armrests at an accessible price, ideal for first-time buyers and secondary desk setups.

## Features

- **4D Adjustable Armrests**: Height, angle, depth, and rotation adjustments
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Durable, easy-to-clean surface
- **High-Density Foam**: Firm support for extended sessions
- **Removable Cushions**: Lumbar and neck pillow support
- **Reclining Function**: Up to 155° tilt with lock
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

Starting at approximately $159.99

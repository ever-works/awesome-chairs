## Overview

The Staples Driscott Ergonomic Fabric Managers Big & Tall Chair is designed for users who need extra room and higher weight capacity. Its seat is wider and deeper than most standard office chairs, making it an instant hit with testers above average in height and weight.

## Features

- **Extra-wide seat**: Measures 23.6 inches wide by 19.9 inches deep, a couple of inches larger than standard picks
- **High weight capacity**: Supports up to 400 pounds (same as the Steelcase Gesture)
- **Plush, comfortable seat**: Felt plushy and comfortable to testers
- **Adjustments**: Seat goes up and down, arms go up and down, lumbar support has a depth dial
- **Cross-legged friendly**: Larger seat accommodates testers who like to sit cross-legged at their desks

## Flaws and Limitations

- **Minimal armrest padding**: Armrests were not as comfortable as other chairs due to minimal padding
- **Limited adjustments**: Only basic height adjustments for seat and arms; lumbar support depth dial did not make a noticeable difference in testing
- **Short return window**: Staples' furniture return window is only 14 days

## Pricing

- Budget-friendly price point (significantly less than premium picks like the Steelcase Gesture or Herman Miller Aeron)

## Warranty

- Limited 5-year warranty

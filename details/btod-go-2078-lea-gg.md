## Overview

The BTOD GO-2078-LEA-GG is a faux leather office chair designed for big and tall users, featuring LeatherSoft hybrid upholstery made from recycled leather blended with vinyl. It has a 28\" high back with a built-in headrest and is 24/7 rated.

## Key Specifications

- **Weight Capacity:** 500 lbs.
- **Seat Width:** 22\" wide
- **Back Height:** 28\" with built-in headrest
- **Starting Price:** ~$593.99

## Features

- LeatherSoft upholstery (recycled leather blended with vinyl) for a soft feel at reduced cost
- 28\" high back design with built-in headrest
- Oversized cushions for a cushy seating experience
- Padded arms with matching faux leather
- 24/7 rated for continuous use

## Best For

Users looking for a leather-feel chair at an accessible price point. Good for those who prefer the feel of leather without the premium cost of genuine leather.

## Considerations

- The 22\" wide seat may not comfortably fit users weighing near the 500 lb. capacity
- Headrest positioning may hit shoulders rather than neck on some users depending on height
- Two-year warranty on all components — shorter than higher-end options may not be ideal for long-term investment

# ProtoArc EC100

Budget-friendly office chair featuring a classic design with thinner plastic frame and plastic casters. Highlighted in comparisons for its affordability compared to premium models like Herman Miller Embody, suitable for general use but less durable for extended hours. Offers standard adjustability typical of mid-range options.

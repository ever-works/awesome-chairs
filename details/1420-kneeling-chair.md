## Overview

The 1420 is one of the most common kneeling chairs, sold under several brand names including Flash Furniture (WL-1420-GG), Office Star Products (KCM1420), and BetterPosture (Jobri F1420). Despite its widespread availability, reviewers do not recommend purchasing it.

## Features

- X-shaped metal base with four wheels
- Hand crank seat height adjustment
- Two separate knee pads
- Sold by multiple manufacturers at similar price points
- Strong warranty from manufacturers

## Pros

- Strong warranty
- Hand crank seat height adjustment

## Cons

- Low-end build quality (defective units common: misaligned screw holes, crooked frame and knee pads)
- Poor sitting mobility (casters attach at angles, can only roll in one direction, must drag more than roll)
- Uncomfortable knee/shin padding
- Uncomfortable seat (small padding, low-quality fabric)
- No range of motion while seated
- 200 lbs weight limit
- Only comfortable for about 20-30 minutes at a time

## Specifications

- Base type: X-shaped metal frame with 4 wheels
- Weight limit: 200 lbs
- Seat height adjustment: Hand crank

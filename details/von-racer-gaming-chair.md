## Overview

The Von Racer Gaming Chair is one of the most affordable pink gaming chairs available, typically priced around $129. It features a full pink PU leather design with white accents around the sides.

## Features

- Full pink PU leather construction with white accents
- Extendable, foldable padded footrest
- USB electric massage pillow (lumbar)
- 135° maximum recline angle
- Racer-back ergonomic design
- Ergonomic form factor with comfortable cushioning

## Dimensions

- Seat depth: 18.9\" 
- Seat width: 18.1\"
- Height: 48.8\" - 52\"
- Max weight capacity: 250 lbs

## Pricing

Typically available for around $129, making it one of the cheapest pink gaming chairs with extra features.

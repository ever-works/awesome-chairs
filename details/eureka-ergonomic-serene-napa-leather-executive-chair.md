## Overview

The Eureka Ergonomic Serene is a Napa leather executive office chair designed for professionals seeking a blend of premium aesthetics and ergonomic comfort.

## Features

- **Napa leather**: Soft and supple genuine leather for long-lasting comfort and professional appearance
- **Adjustable lumbar support**: Independent height and depth adjustment for precise lower back positioning
- **Adjustable headrest**: Multi-directional head and neck support
- **Smooth tilt mechanism**: Recline with tilt lock and tension control
- **Thick high-rebound cushion**: Maintains shape and comfort over time
- **Aluminum alloy base**: Durable foundation with smooth-rolling casters

## Build Quality

- Premium materials throughout including genuine Napa leather
- Thick seat and back padding with high-density foam
- Aluminum base with nylon casters

## Target Users

- Professionals who want executive aesthetics with ergonomic adjustability
- Users who prefer genuine leather over mesh for a traditional feel

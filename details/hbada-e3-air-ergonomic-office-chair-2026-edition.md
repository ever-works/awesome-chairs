- **3-Zone Floating Wing Lumbar Support System**:
  - 5cm Height Adjustment (6 Levels)
  - 3cm forward and backward (4 Levels)
  - 40° Side Wing Rotation (3 Levels)
  - 12° Spring-Based Adaptive Support

- **360° Omni-Motion Mech-Armrests**:
  - Armrest pads Rotation: 360°
  - Height Adjustment: 70mm
  - Forward/Backward: 100mm
  - 40° Synchronized Recline Integration

- **4D Dual-Axis Adjustable Headrest**:

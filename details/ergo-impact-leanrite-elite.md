## Overview

The LeanRite Elite is a standing chair that features a dual stage height adjustment system giving it a massive height adjustment range. The seat can tilt straight up and down, allowing you to rest against it with your lower back when fully standing. It is the only standing chair tested that allows leaning while fully standing.

## Features

- Dual-stage height adjustment system for massive height range
- Unique seat tilt function — tilts to straight up and down
- Built-in footrest with anti-fatigue mat
- Integral skin seat material — durable and easy to clean
- Good build quality
- Strong warranty

## Pros

- Unique seat tilt function
- Huge seat height range
- Good build quality
- Good range of motion
- Strong warranty
- Anti-fatigue mat included
- May be good option for longer hours

## Cons

- Takes up a lot of space due to built-in footrest
- Seat may be uncomfortable — fairly small and firm
- Poor sitting mobility
- Poor portability

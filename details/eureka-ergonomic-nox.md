## Overview

The Eureka Ergonomic Nox Chair represents a leap in the brand's design language, departing from gaming-chair aesthetics to deliver a refined, modern executive chair. It features genuine leather upholstery and a striking silhouette with a well-concealed metal structure.

## Design & Build

- Clean, minimalist design language
- Silhouette-tapered lumbar support that is bold without being flashy
- Sweeping, well-shaped armrests
- Full-metal seat base (not molded plastic)
- Clean metal-to-metal attachment points where the arms and seat meet the backrest
- Good cable management with a hidden wire channel from the seat base

## Features

- Height and angle adjustable armrests with a soft-touch top surface
- Good build quality relative to gaming and budget ergonomic chairs
- Seat reclines up to 135°
- Lumbar support that feels bold but subtle in design

## Target Users

- Professionals who want a modern, executive-looking chair
- Users seeking premium genuine leather with a clean metal construction rather than a gaming-chair aesthetic
- Those comfortable with a more minimalist design approach compared to traditional executive chairs

## Pricing

Priced around $260, making it an accessible premium option compared to leather task chairs from traditional office brands that often exceed $700.

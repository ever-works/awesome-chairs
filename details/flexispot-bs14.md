## Overview

The FlexiSpot BS14 is a budget-friendly ergonomic chair that provides essential ergonomic features at an accessible price point.

## Features

- Adjustable lumbar support
- Breathable mesh back for airflow
- Height and angle adjustable armrests
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment
- 125-degree recline with tension control

## Specifications

- Recline Range: Up to 125 degrees
- Warranty: 3-year manufacturer warranty
- Price: Starting at $159.99

## Best For

- Budget-conscious users seeking basic ergonomic features
- Home office workers needing adjustable lumbar support and mesh ventilation
- Users who want a decent recline without premium chair costs

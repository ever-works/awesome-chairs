## Overview

The FlexiSpot Ergonomic Office Chair BS14 is a cost-friendly and comfortable office chair that has plenty of adjustable features to maximize comfort. Recent reviews note that it's so comfortable and easy to maneuver that users actually prefer to work at their desk.

## Features

- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable lower back support
- **Recline Function**: Up to 125-degree recline angle
- **Breathable Mesh Back**: Promotes airflow for all-day comfort
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Height and angle adjustable arms
- **Seat Height Adjustment**: Pneumatic height adjustment
- **Smooth-Rolling Casters**: Easy mobility on various floor types

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 250 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 125°
- Seat Height Range: 18" to 22"
- Warranty: 3-year manufacturer warranty

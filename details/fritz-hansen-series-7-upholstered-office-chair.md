## Overview

The Series 7 Chair — also called Model 3107 — is Arne Jacobsen's legendary creation from 1955, the best-selling chair in Fritz Hansen's history and one of the most recognized pieces of Danish design worldwide.

## Features

- Laminated veneer shell with soft fabric upholstery
- Steel base with chrome finish
- Height-adjustable via gas lift
- Compact footprint ideal for collaborative spaces
- Available in various textile colors
- Optional castors for mobility
- Manufactured with sustainable forestry-certified veneer

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: 242 lbs (110 kg)
- Seat Height Range: Adjustable via pneumatic lift
- Dimensions: Compact and versatile footprint

## Overview

The Secretlab Titan Evo Nanogen XL is the big and tall variant of Secretlab's top-rated Titan Evo Nanogen gaming chair. It takes everything from the standard Nanogen edition and upsizes it to deliver comfort and support to a wider, larger audience.

## Specifications

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Seat Height | 18.1-21.9" |
| Seat Width | 19.3" |
| Seat Depth | 19.7" |
| Backrest Length | 35" |
| Backrest Width | 22" |
| Tilt Range | 85-165° |
| Max Load | 395 lbs |

## Key Features

- **Nanogen Hybrid Leatherette**: Ultra-soft, Brisa-like fabric that is significantly softer and more comfortable than standard PU leather
- **Extra seat cushion**: Additional Nanofoam Composite cushioning for extra softness while maintaining support
- **Adjustable lumbar support**: Delivers proper back support critical for larger users
- **4D adjustable armrests**: Hot-swappable top cushions with magnetic attachment; defaults with velour-wrapped PlushCell foam arm toppers
- **Magnetic head pillow**: PlushCell magnetic neck pillow for enhanced comfort
- **Memory foam with cooling gel**: On headrest and armrest components
- **Sturdy aluminum wheelbase**: Upgraded from plastic for enhanced durability
- **Magnetic accessory compatibility**: Full compatibility with Secretlab's magnetic accessories including pillows, armrest covers, and the Ergonomic Recliner add-on

## Pros

- Wide, well-cushioned seat for larger frames
- Soft and comfortable yet still supportive
- Sturdy aluminum wheelbase
- Accessory ecosystem compatibility

## Cons

- Pricier than the smaller standard model

## Pricing

The Titan Evo Nanogen XL comes at a premium over the standard-sized Nanogen model, reflecting its larger dimensions and materials. It is positioned as a high-end gaming chair investment.

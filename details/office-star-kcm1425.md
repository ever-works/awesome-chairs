## Overview

The Office Star KCM1425 is the next closest kneeling chair to a standard office chair after the Jobri Jazzy. It features a 5-star base with smooth-rolling wheels and pneumatic seat adjustment, though it suffers from low-end build quality and shin comfort issues.

## Features

- 5-star base with smoothly functioning wheels
- Pneumatic cylinder controlled by lever under the seat
- Large seat with adequate padding
- Strong warranty from Office Star

## Pros

- Strong warranty
- Good sitting mobility
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment (easier than hand-screw models)

## Cons

- Low-end build quality
- Poor knee/shin comfort (nearly flat seat angle makes it hard to keep shins against pads)
- Very limited range of motion (seat swivel only)
- 200 lbs weight limit
- Thin, stretchy fabric may not be durable
- May not be comfortable for long hours

## Specifications

- Base type: 5-star with wheels
- Weight limit: 200 lbs
- Seat height adjustment: Pneumatic lever

## Pricing

Approximately $209.34 with free shipping.

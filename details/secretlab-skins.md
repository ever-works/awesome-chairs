## Overview

Secretlab SKINS are custom design covers for the Secretlab TITAN Evo gaming chair. They allow owners to personalize their chairs with designs from favorite franchises and media properties while adding an extra layer of protection.

## Features

- Wide variety of licensed franchise and themed designs available
- New designs released regularly to expand selection
- Precision-cut for Titan Evo fit — tool-free, snap-on installation
- Provides an extra protective layer against spills, pet hair, and UV fading
- Compatible with Titan Evo base models (Small, Regular, XL)
- Adds a custom aesthetic while preserving the original upholstery underneath

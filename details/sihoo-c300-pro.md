# Sihoo C300 PRO - Ergonomic Office Chair

## Overview
The **Sihoo C300 PRO** is an advanced ergonomic office chair designed for professionals who spend long hours at a desk.

## Features
- **Multi-Dimensional Headrest**: Adjustable in height, depth, and angle for neck support
- **Adaptive Lumbar Support**: Auto-adjusting lower-back support that follows your posture
- **Full Mesh Backrest**: Breathable design for all-day cooling
- **3D Adjustable Armrests**: Height, width, and angle adjustment
- **Waterfall Seat Edge**: Reduces thigh pressure during extended sitting
- **Tilt Lock**: Multiple recline positions with tension control

## Specifications
- Weight Capacity: 300 lbs
- Warranty: 2-Year Manufacturer Warranty
- Seat Height: 17.7"–21.6"

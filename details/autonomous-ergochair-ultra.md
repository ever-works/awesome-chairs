## Overview

The Autonomous ErgoChair Ultra is designed to comfortably accommodate larger frames, with testing confirming comfort for users up to 6-foot-1 and 270 pounds. It features an extremely comfortable seat pad and noticeable lumbar support.

## Features

- Solid metal wheel hub construction to prevent stretching and floor scraping over time (unlike nylon hubs found on some chairs)
- Extremely comfortable, well-padded seat
- Strong, palpable lumbar support
- Breathable fabric that keeps the back cool during extended use
- Supports users up to 300 pounds
- Easy assembly with solid nuts and bolts

## Cons

- Wheels are weak for a 300-pound-rated chair and do not glide well; may need upgrading to roller blade-style wheels for carpeted surfaces
- Armrests do not twist, which can make them less comfortable compared to chairs with fully adjustable arms

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing — positioned as a value option for its target audience.

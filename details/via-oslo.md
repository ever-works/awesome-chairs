## Overview

The Via Oslo is a high-back executive chair with a 31" tall backrest, inspired by automotive engineering. It features a steel frame and injection molded foam, commanding a room with its presence. It is one of the most customizable chairs available, with hundreds of fabric options.

## Features

- 31" tall backrest
- Steel frame with injection molded foam
- Standard knee-tilt mechanism
- Ratchet back height adjustment (rare on chairs in this style)
- Optional seat slider
- Optional synchro tilt mechanism
- Eight different armrest options, including 4-way arm cap adjustment
- Hundreds of fabric options in varying colors, patterns, and durability levels
- Can be designed with different fabrics on seat and back
- 21" wide seat

## Limitations

- High price tag, starting at $1,776.00 and ranging up to $3,500+
- Large dimensions (31" back height, 21" seat width) may be uncomfortable for petite users
- Not affordable for a large percentage of buyers

## Pricing

Starting at $1,776.00 with free shipping, with fully customized configurations reaching $3,500+.

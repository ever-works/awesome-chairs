## Overview

The BTOD GO-99-3-GG is a big and tall mesh office chair designed to support users up to 500 lbs in round-the-clock applications. It features beefed-up components throughout and a generously sized 25.5" wide seat suitable for taller and larger users.

## Features

- 500 lbs weight capacity
- 25.5" wide seat with seat height range suited for tall people
- Rated for 24/7 usage
- Reinforced casters, cylinder, chair base, and arms
- Swivel tilt mechanism with tilt lock and tension adjustment
- Height adjustable arms
- Height adjustable backrest
- Fully adjustable lumbar support (up/down and in/out)
- Mesh back design

## Potential Considerations

- Only suitable for big and tall users; the seat, back, and arm width are too large for average-sized people
- Arms do not have adjustability beyond height and are too far apart for most average users
- No seat slider to adjust the seat depth
- Lower quality components manufactured in China with a short warranty period
- Mesh is not as soft or flexible as higher-end products

## Overview

The Eurotech iOO is a highly adjustable ergonomic chair that offers more adjustability than the popular Ergohuman. It is designed as an affordable alternative to premium high-end models while maintaining good mesh quality and functionality.

## Features

- Seat height and depth adjustment
- Synchro-tilt mechanism with tension control
- Four-position tilt lock
- Back height adjustment
- Weight-sensitive lumbar adjustment
- 4D arms (height, width, depth, and pivot adjustment)
- Optional adjustable headrest
- Available with black or white frame
- Choice of mesh or padded fabric seat
- Great lumbar support highly rated in office testing

## Potential Considerations

- The backrest does not support a fully upright position; it remains at a slight recline at its most forward angle
- Minimum seat height is 20", so users under 5'9" may need a footrest
- Build quality is not on par with premium chairs like the Aeron or CXO, though still good for the price

## Overview

Vitrazza produces a tempered glass chair mat designed to replace traditional plastic or vinyl mats. It protects floors from caster wheel damage and improves chair movement on both hard floors and carpets.

## Features

- Made from thick safety glass, resistant to scratches after years of use
- Supports up to 1,000 pounds
- Rubber bumpers keep the mat from directly contacting hardwood floors
- Available in multiple sizes and shapes (rectangular and other options)
- Does not scuff or chip hardwood surfaces

## Pricing

Premium-priced compared to traditional plastic or vinyl mats. Exact pricing varies by size and shape selected.

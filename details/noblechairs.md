## Overview
Noblechairs designs premium gaming and office chairs for people who spend long hours at a desk or computer. The brand focuses on ergonomic design, high-quality materials, and long-term seating comfort, drawing on design principles used in high-class car seat manufacturing.

## Features
- **Product Types & Series**
  - LEGEND Series gaming chairs
  - HERO Series gaming chairs
  - HERO ST Series
  - EPIC Series gaming chairs
  - EPIC Compact Series (smaller form factor)
  - ICON Series
  - Special and licensed editions (e.g., DOOM The Dark Ages, Starfield, e-sports editions)
  - Accessories such as pillow sets

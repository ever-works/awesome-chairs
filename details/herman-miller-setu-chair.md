## Overview

The Herman Miller Setu is an ergonomic chair designed by Formway Design, featuring a kinematic spine that flexes to conform to the user's body.

## Features

- Kinematic spinal support system
- Adjustable arms (height and width)
- Tilt tension control
- Pneumatic seat height adjustment
- Flexible suspension back
- Simple adjustment mechanism

## Warranty

Comes with Herman Miller's standard 12-year warranty.

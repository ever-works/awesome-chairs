## Overview

The Eureka Ergonomic Mathias is a premium executive office chair that features high-quality Napa leather, Italian minimalist style, and FSC-certified wood veneers for an elegant, refined aesthetic.

## Features

- FSC-certified wood veneers — sustainable, beautifully finished surface accent
- Premium Napa leather — soft, supple leather for a luxurious feel
- Italian minimalist design style
- Ergonomic padding for all-day comfort
- Professional and elegant aesthetic suitable for home workspaces, executive offices, board rooms, and management offices
- Sturdy construction

## Color Options

- Classic Brown
- Modern Black

## Target Users

Well-suited for users who want premium aesthetics and comfortable leather seating rather than mesh ergonomic task seating.

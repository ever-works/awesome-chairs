## Overview

The GTPLAYER Footrest Series LR002 is an affordable gaming chair designed with budget-conscious gamers in mind. Its integrated footrest and essential ergonomic features make it a cost-effective option for casual use and secondary desk setups.

## Features

- **Integrated Footrest**: Retractable for leg support during relaxation
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Durable and easy-to-clean surface
- **Height-Adjustable Armrests**: Customizable arm positioning
- **Reclining Function**: Up to 155° tilt with lock mechanism
- **Removable Cushions**: Lumbar support and neck pillow
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

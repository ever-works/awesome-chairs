## Overview

The SL5000 features an independently adjustable backrest, allowing you to adjust the angle of your sitting position up to 140 degrees. It's upgradable with a compatible RGB LED upgrade kit with wireless controls, interactive modes, and 16.8 million colors.

## Features

- **Independently Adjustable Backrest**: Up to 140-degree recline
- **RGB LED Compatible**: Upgradable with wireless RGB lighting kit
- **Patented Slide-in Hardware**: Quick one-person assembly in seconds
- **PUC Material**: Combines PVC durability with PU softness
- **Memory Foam Cushions**: Easily removable neck and lumbar support
- **Premium Build Quality**: High-grade materials and construction

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 330 lbs
- Recline Range: 90° to 140°
- Warranty: 2-year manufacturer warranty

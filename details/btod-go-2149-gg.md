## Overview

The BTOD GO-2149-GG is a durable fabric big and tall office chair with a 24" wide seat and 400 lb. capacity. It features a generous seat height range making it well-suited for taller users. At approximately 30" wide and 34" deep overall, it's a substantial chair built for comfort.

## Key Specifications

- **Weight Capacity:** 400 lbs.
- **Seat Width:** 24" wide
- **Seat Height Range:** 20"–24"
- **Overall Dimensions:** 30" wide, 34" deep
- **Weight:** ~70 lbs.
- **Starting Price:** ~$542.99

## Features

- 4" thick seat pad with waterfall front edge to promote healthy blood flow
- Height-adjustable arms
- Durable fabric upholstery
- Seat height range of 20" to 24" — ideal for users over 6'4" tall
- Large, substantial build

## Best For

Tall users (over 6'4") who need a high seat height range and a wide, spacious seat with durable fabric upholstery around the $600 price range.

## Considerations

- Two-year warranty on the entire chair — shorter than higher-end options
- Minimum seat height of 20" may be too tall for shorter users; a footrest may be needed
- Heavy at nearly 70 lbs., requiring extra hands for moving and assembly

## Overview

The SL4000 gaming chair brings unparalleled levels of ergonomic comfort and adjustability, crafted with high-density padding and a contoured backrest for longer gaming sessions. As the middle child of the S-Line, it offers a balanced combination of aesthetics with ergonomics.

## Features

- **High-Density Padding**: Extra cushioning for extended comfort
- **Contoured Backrest**: Ergonomically shaped for natural spinal support
- **Memory Foam Cushions**: Removable neck and lumbar support pillows
- **4-Directional Armrests**: Ergonomically designed adjustable arms
- **Industrial-Grade Gas Lift**: Class-4 gas lift for reliable height adjustment
- **140° Recline**: Generous recline range for relaxation

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 330 lbs

- Aluminum Alloy Base: Five-star base for stability and durability
- Warranty: 2-year manufacturer warranty

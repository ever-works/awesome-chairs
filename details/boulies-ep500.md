## Overview

The Boulies EP500 is designed specifically for extended work sessions, providing consistent support to maintain posture throughout the workday. It is a chiropractor-appointed model engineered based on the expert requirements of Boulies' in-house chiropractors.

## Key Features

- **Dynamic Lumbar Support**: Adapts in real-time to your movements, shifting along with you to provide optimal back support at every position
- **Foldable 5D Armrests**: Full range of adjustments including height, pad rotation, flip-up/fold-down functionality, and front-to-back sliding; armrests can be flipped up to create a spacious seating area for cross-legged sitting
- **Adjustable Seat Depth**: Ensures proper tailbone support and promotes ergonomic alignment for different leg lengths
- **Backrest tilt angles**: Adjustable at 98°, 110°, and 126°
- **Generous 50 cm seat width** for users who find standard office chairs too narrow
- **Integrated footrest** to support legs during recline and break periods
- **Mesh version available** recommended for users who perspire easily

## Best For

- Individuals who sit for long periods and tend to shift positions frequently (sitting cross-legged, reclining)
- A wide variety of work activities including sewing, typing, reading, and taking short breaks
- Users of average to tall stature (165–190 cm)
- Those who want dynamic lumbar support and 5D armrests — features typically found only on premium chairs over $1,000 from brands like Steelcase or Herman Miller

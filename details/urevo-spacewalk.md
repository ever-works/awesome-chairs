## Overview

The Urevo SpaceWalk is an under-desk treadmill designed to pair with standing desk setups, enabling users to walk while working or gaming. It features a slim, wheeled design for easy storage and portability.

## Features

- Compact dimensions (120x48x12cm total) with 100x38cm usable walking surface
- Wooden trim aesthetic to blend into office environments
- LED segment display for monitoring activity
- Companion mobile app for tracking and control
- Remote control for basic functionality without smartphone
- Lightweight design with wheels for easy storage
- Max speed of 4mph

## Specifications

- Rated max weight: 120kg/265lbs
- Walking surface: 100x38cm

## Pricing

Available through Urevo Store and Amazon. Positioned as an affordable treadmill option for desk use.

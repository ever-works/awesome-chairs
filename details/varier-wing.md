## Overview

The Varier Wing is an unconventional working chair that combines kneeling chair ergonomics with the functionality of a traditional swivel office chair. Its height adjustment, swivel, and tilt functions make it versatile for any workspace.

## Features

- Height-adjustable design ideal for any desk or table height, even above standard heights
- 360-degree swivel function for easy workspace mobility
- Tilt functions for dynamic movement
- Castor wheels available for hard or soft floors
- Promotes active sitting and deep breathing
- Boosts energy levels and concentration
- Forward-tilt capability for proper spinal alignment
- Designed to prevent prolonged static sitting

## Use Cases

Ideal for professionals who want kneeling chair posture benefits combined with the convenience of a traditional adjustable office chair. Especially well-suited for users with non-standard desk heights.

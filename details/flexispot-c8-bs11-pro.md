## Overview

The FlexiSpot C8 BS11 Pro is a versatile all-mesh office chair with a curved seat base, adjustable lumbar support, adjustable headrest, and back tilt with armrest adjustments.

## Features

- All-mesh construction for maximum breathability
- Curved seat base design
- Adjustable lumbar support via curved back
- Adjustable headrest
- Synchronized back tilt and armrest movement
- Weight capacity: 130 kg (286 lbs)
- 5-year warranty

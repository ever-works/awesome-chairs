## Overview

The Office Master OM5 is a self-weighing task chair designed for intuitive adjustment based on user weight and size, eliminating the need for manual levers.

## Features

- Polyflex back and fabric seat
- Seat height adjustment: 37–64 cm (14.7–25 inch)
- Weight capacity: 136 kg (300 lbs)
- Chair weight: 29 kg (64 lbs)
- Available colors: Black, Grey, White
- Automatic tension adjustments for backrest and seat pan
- Smooth articulation with wheels on runners for transitioning between postures
- Manual adjustments available for further customization

## Pricing

Mid-range pricing with high-end quality. Availability primarily in the US; import fees apply elsewhere.

## Use Cases

Suitable for multi-user environments, long work or gaming sessions, and users seeking fuss-free ergonomic support.

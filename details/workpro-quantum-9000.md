## Overview

The WorkPro Quantum 9000 Series is a mid-range ergonomic desk chair often compared to the Herman Miller Aeron due to their similar appearance. At its price point, it's considered one of the most well-built, comfortable mid-back mesh chairs available.

## Features

- **Adjustable Synchro-Tilt**: Synchronized seat and backrest movement
- **Independent Controls**: Separate seat and back height adjustments
- **Premium Lumbar Cushioning**: Adjustable lumbar support
- **Waterfall-Edge Seat**: Refined seat edge for improved circulation
- **Breathable Mesh**: Promotes airflow to keep you cool
- **Fully Adjustable Armrests**: Height, width, and angle adjustable

## Specifications

- Weight Capacity: Up to 275 lbs
- Recommended Use: 8-10 hours per day
- Certifications: BIFMA compliant
- Available Configurations: Mid-back and high-back versions
- Warranty: Limited manufacturer warranty

## Overview

The IKEA Jarvfjallet is an affordable ergonomic office chair priced at approximately $300. It includes a padded headrest with height adjustment and adjustable armrests.

## Features

- Padded headrest with height adjustment
- Adjustable armrests (height)
- Adjustable seat height and tilt
- Foam-padded seat and backrest
- Available in multiple upholstery options
- Weight capacity: up to 243 lbs

## Limitations

- Lacks ergonomic curve for lumbar support
- Standard adjustment points only (no seat depth, no 4D arms)
- Build quality is modest compared to premium alternatives

## Pricing

Approximately $300 at IKEA retail stores and online.

# Steelcase Series 2 with Headrest

The Steelcase Series 2 with Headrest adds a generously padded, recline-focused headrest to the Series 2's flexor-system ergonomic frame, providing comfortable neck support when leaning back during breaks or phone calls. It rates users up to 300 lbs and shares the base chair's adjustable lumbar and seat mechanics, making it suitable for standard office desk use. Compared to the Steelcase Leap's headrest option, it offers a flatter, wider pillow preferred by some reclinement users, though it tends to drop position more easily during active tasking, trading stability for a softer feel.

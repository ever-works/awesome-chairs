## Overview

The Ohaho Gaming Chair is an affordable gaming chair designed for casual gamers and budget-conscious users who want basic racing-chair features without premium pricing.

## Features

- **Racing-Style Design**: Sporty gaming aesthetic
- **PU Leather Upholstery**: Easy-to-clean surface
- **Removable Cushions**: Lumbar and neck pillow support
- **Fixed Armrests**: Basic padded arm support
- **Reclining Function**: Up to 145° tilt with lock mechanism
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Standard gas lift

Starting at approximately $109.99

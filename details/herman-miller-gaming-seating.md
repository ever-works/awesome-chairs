# Herman Miller Gaming Seating

![Herman Miller Logo](https://www.hermanmiller.com/content/dam/hmicom/page_assets/homepage/primary-nav/herman-miller-logo.svg)

### Overview
Performance-focused chairs for gaming and work, including Embody Gaming, Vantum, Aeron Gaming, Sayl Gaming.

### Features
- **Ergonomic Support**: Body-adapting designs reduce strain.
- **Adjustability**: Armrests, recline (135-155°), tilt tension.
- **Materials**: Breathable suspension, cooling foams.
- **Capacity**: 300+ lbs per model.

### Use Cases
- Extended gaming, streaming, office hybrids.

### Pricing
Model-dependent; Embody ~$2,000+, Vantum ~$1,000+. Check official site.

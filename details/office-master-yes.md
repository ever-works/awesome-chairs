## Headrest Review

The Office Master Yes chair features one of the few flat headrest designs that reviewers found genuinely usable and comfortable.

## Headrest Features

- **Height and depth adjustment**: Slides along the frame for both vertical and forward/backward positioning
- **Flat profile**: One of the few flatter headrest designs that works well for reclining, lounging, and watching content
- **Neck curve compatibility**: Can be turned to fit into the curve of the neck
- **Moderate padding**: Comfortable without being overly large or soft
- **Surprising range**: More adjustability than expected for this design type

## Expert Ratings

- Consensus: **B-Tier** (with some debate between C+ and A-)
- The headrest's angle doesn't always hold firmly, showing a small amount of play/movement
- One of the better flat headrest designs available

## Drawbacks

- Height adjustment slides on the frame and may not hold its position firmly
- Not super padded
- May not provide as much head support as deeper, more aggressive headrest designs

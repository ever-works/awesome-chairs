## Haworth Soji Ergonomic Office Chair

The **Haworth Soji** is a premium ergonomic office chair with comprehensive adjustability for extended seated work.

## Features
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable lower-back alignment
- **4-Dimensional Armrests**: Height, width, depth, and pivot
- **Seat Depth Adjustment**: Adapts to different leg lengths
- **Waterfall Seat Edge**: Reduces pressure on thighs and knees
- **Robust warranty**: Industry-leading coverage with free returns

## Haworth Zody II

The **Haworth Zody II** is an advanced ergonomic task chair with upgraded upright seating support and refined adjustability.

## Features
- **3-Point Synchronous Tilt System**: Aligns with natural body pivot points
- **Asymmetrical Lumbar Support**: Personalized comfort with independent left/right adjustment
- **Pelvic Support System**: Consistent lower-back support
- **Adaptive Suspension Mesh**: Three distinct ergonomic zones
- **Torsional Flex Technology**: Dynamic backrest response to shifts in position
- **4D Adjustable Arms**: Height, width, depth, and pivot
- **Seat Depth Adjustment**: 16.8"–19.5" (with lumbar); 17.5"–20.2" (without)

## Sustainability
- Cradle to Cradle certified; GREENGUARD Gold certified

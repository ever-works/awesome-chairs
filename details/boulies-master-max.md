## Overview

Executive big & tall chair for extended sitting with premium materials.

## Features

- 150kg (330lbs) capacity
- Extended wide seat/depth
- 4D aluminum armrests
- Adjustable lumbar pillow
- Cold-cure foam cushion
- 95°-165° recline + rocking
- Optional leg rest

## Use Cases

Supports programmers during 10+ hour coding marathons with posture focus. Ideal for executives needing robust daily office comfort. Fits larger users in hybrid work/gaming without bottoming out.

## Comparisons

Vs Titan Evo: Wider seat and leg rest for big users; similar recline but more office-oriented lumbar.

## Overview

The Boulies Master Max is designed as an executive/big and tall option, offering extended seat width and height with a 150kg weight capacity. It is built for larger frame users who require a robust base and reliable support during short to moderate sitting sessions.

## Key Features

- **Extended seat width and height** for larger users
- **150kg weight capacity** with reinforced construction
- **4D adjustable armrests** with aluminum construction for multi-axis adjustment
- **Customizable integrated lumbar pillow** that adjusts up and down to match the natural curvature of the spine
- **Cold-cure foam seat cushion** that resists bottoming out during use
- **Tailored round-edge cushion** to eliminate sharp edges and reduce pressure on hips and thighs
- **Rocking/reclining mechanism** with 95°-165° recline range and 15° rocking flexibility
- **Optional built-in leg rest** for reclining breaks
- **Class-4 gas lift** and heavy-duty base for durability
- **Available materials**: Ultraflex PU leather or water-repellent fabric

## Best For

- Large-frame users needing more robust build quality and weight capacity
- Users who spend under 8 hours seated and need reliable day-after-day support
- Those looking for high-quality materials with an executive look and feel

## Considerations

- Recommended for short to moderate sessions rather than prolonged 8+ hour use
- Premium pricing relative to value models
- Leather option may feel warmer than mesh in hot climates — fabric/mesh variants recommended for users who sweat easily
- Sizeable chair footprint that may take up more room

## Overview

The Corsair WW T2 Road Warrior is a mid-range gaming chair designed for comfort during extended gaming sessions, featuring a perforated PU leather design for improved breathability.

## Features

- Perforated design on back and seat cushioning for breathability
- Reclines up to 170°
- Tilt mechanism with 0-17° range and tilt lock
- 4D adjustable armrests
- Aluminum base reinforced with high-grade steel skeleton frame
- Rollerblade-style wheels suitable for hardwood, carpet, and laminate floors
- Available in multiple color schemes: red/black, blue, yellow, white, and black
- Includes removable neck and lumbar support pillows
- 2-year manufacturer warranty

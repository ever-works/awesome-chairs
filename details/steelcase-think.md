## Overview

The Steelcase Think is an ergonomic chair priced around $1,100, designed with a highly flexible backrest and seat pad design. It is positioned as a chair best suited for shorter sitting sessions, making it ideal for conference rooms and offices where extended eight-plus hour use is less common.

## Features

- Highly flexible backrest for natural movement
- Flexible seat pad design similar to the Karman
- Four-way adjustable armrests similar to the Leap, offering comfortable arm support
- Lightweight construction
- Clean, modern aesthetic

## Considerations

- Better suited for shorter sitting sessions rather than all-day, eight-plus hour use
- Less padding in the seat compared to Steelcase's higher-end models
- Minimal lower back support compared to the Leap V2 or Karman

## Overview

The LEMBERI Charles is an affordable gaming chair designed to deliver quality comfort without the premium price tag. It combines a racing-style aesthetic with functional ergonomic features, making it suitable for extended gaming sessions.

## Specifications

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Weight Capacity | Up to 400 lbs |
| Chair Dimensions | 24" Depth, 27.2" Width, 52.9" Height |
| Materials | High-Density Shaping Foam, Skin-Friendly PU Leather |
| Frame & Base | Thick Steel Frame, Steel Base |
| Backrest Reclinability | 90° to 155° |
| Special Features | High Backrest Lumbar Support, Retractable Footrest, Racing Style Design |

## Features

- Built-in lumbar support for improved posture during long sessions
- Retractable footrest — a rare feature at this price point
- High-density shaping foam cushioned seat for lasting comfort
- Thick steel frame and steel base for durability
- Reclines up to 155 degrees for relaxation
- Skin-friendly PU leather upholstery
- Easy assembly with clear instructions

## Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Affordable price tag under $100
- Steel frame and base provide solid support
- Built-in lumbar support for better posture
- Cushioned seat offers comfort for long hours

**Cons:**

Available for under $100, making it one of the most budget-conscious gaming chairs without compromising on core features.

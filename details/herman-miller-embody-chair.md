## Herman Miller Embody Chair

### Features
- **Backfit Adjustment**: Customizes to the natural curve of your spine for personalized support
- **Ergonomic Design**: Promotes healthy sitting posture, reducing strain and fatigue during extended periods
- **Pixel-Matrix Backrest**: Evenly distributes body weight and reduces pressure on the spine
- **Adjustable Arms**: Height and width adjustable
- **Seat Depth Adjustment**: Slides forward or backward

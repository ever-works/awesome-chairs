## Overview

Office Logix Shop is a curated retailer of high-quality office chair accessories designed to improve ergonomic support, comfort, and productivity for desk workers. Their product lineup targets the most common pressure points and comfort gaps in standard office seating.

## Product Categories

- **Lumbar Supports** — Contoured cushions with memory foam or mesh materials that support the natural inward curve of the lumbar spine; many include straps or clips for easy mounting and height-adjustment options for taller torsos.
- **Headrests** — Adjustable in both height and angle to support the cervical spine, reducing neck tension and upper back stiffness; available in brand-specific models for chairs from Herman Miller, Steelcase, and other major manufacturers.
- **Seat Cushions** — Available in memory foam, gel-infused, and hybrid constructions to distribute weight evenly, reduce tailbone pressure, and improve heat dispersion during extended sitting.
- **Armrest Replacements** — Upgraded arm pads with memory foam padding or gel tops, wider surfaces for better pressure distribution, and textured finishes to reduce slip; installation typically requires only a basic screwdriver.
- **Footrests** — Both static elevation models and rocking designs that encourage subtle leg movement; support proper circulation, relieve lower back pressure, and complement setups with non-adjustable desk heights.

## Compatibility

Offers both universal accessories and brand-specific models designed to match the spacing, mount type, and frame of well-known office chair brands. Customers are advised to verify measurements and compatibility before purchasing.

## Quality Standards

Products are tested for durability, ease of use, and ergonomic performance. Materials include high-density foam, reinforced stitching, and sturdy hardware designed to withstand eight or more hours of daily use. Quality seat cushions are rated for 1 to 3 years of service depending on usage and body weight.

## Overview

The Sleekform Atlanta is a budget kneeling chair designed for those seeking thick cushions at a low price. It features an X-base with four casters for mobility, hand crank seat height adjustment, and vinyl upholstery that is easy to clean.

## Features

- X-base design with 4 casters for smooth rolling
- Very thick cushions (though foam is not dense and compresses significantly)
- Vinyl upholstery — smooth to touch and easy to clean
- Hand crank seat height adjustment via screw cylinder under seat

## Pros

- Good sitting mobility
- Hand crank seat height adjustment
- Good portability
- Low cost with thick cushions

## Cons

- Low-end build
- Only a 30-day warranty
- Knee pads and seat may be uncomfortable (padding compresses and bottoms out)
- No range of motion while seated
- Not good for long hours
- Thick padding can engulf you and become warm with vinyl

## Specifications

- Base type: X-frame with 4 casters
- Seat height adjustment: Hand crank screw
- Warranty: 30 days

## Pricing

Approximately $95.00 with free shipping.

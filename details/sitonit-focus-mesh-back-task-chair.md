## Overview

The SitOnIt Focus Mesh Back Task Chair features a breathable mesh back paired with a grey fabric seat, delivering essential ergonomic support in a straightforward, petite-friendly design.

## Features
- Mesh back for ventilation and comfort
- Grey fabric upholstered seat
- Pneumatic height adjustment
- Tilt function for reclining support
- Ergonomically designed frame that fits smaller frames comfortably
- Simple adjustment controls without overwhelming complexity
- Suitable for everyday office work

## Best For
- Users wanting simple, effective ergonomics for petite frames
- Everyday office environments needing reliable seating
- Individuals who prefer straightforward designs over complex adjustments
- Budget-friendly ergonomic seating needs

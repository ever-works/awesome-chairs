## Overview

The OdinLake Upgrade 518 is an ergonomic office chair designed for users who like to change their sitting positions throughout the day. It offers extensive adjustability across all major components and features excellent wheels suitable for any surface, particularly carpeted floors.

## Features

- Fully adjustable arms, back, and lumbar support
- Adjustable seat position and angle
- High-quality wheels that roll well on carpet, rugs, and hard floors
- Solid construction that supports heavier users
- Multiple adjustment points allow users to customize to their preferred sitting style
- Lower lumbar support option with angle-adjustable armrests for typing

## Pricing

Priced lower than competitors like the Ergo Max 747, offering comparable comfort at a fraction of the cost.

## Design

The chair has a strong "office furniture" aesthetic with a clinical, professional look. It may not blend seamlessly with warmer or more decorative home office setups, but it delivers serious ergonomic performance.

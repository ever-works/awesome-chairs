## Overview

The TS-83 Techni-Sport Gaming Chair is a durable, premium pink-accented gaming chair built with a steel frame and memory foam cushions. It features lighter pink accents throughout the chair with a cool gray base color and black frame and armrests.

## Features

- Steel frame construction for durability
- Memory foam cushioning for premium comfort
- 4D armrests adjustable across height, depth, width, and angle
- Faux leather material
- Non-marking casters
- Back recline up to 150°
- Particularly wide seat for larger users
- 3% of all sales go towards fighting breast cancer

## Dimensions

- Seat depth: 25.5"
- Seat width: 29.5"
- Height: 53"
- Max weight capacity: 300 lbs

## Pricing

Positioned as a pricier chair with premium features. The 150° recline is slightly restrictive compared to some competitors.

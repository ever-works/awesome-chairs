## Overview

The AndaSeat Kaiser 3 XL is the best gaming chair for bigger people, offering exceptional value at the $500/£500 price point. Built with a robust steel chassis and strong castor wheels, it accommodates users up to 395lb/180kg in weight and 6'8"/210cm in height.

## Specifications

- **Dimensions:** 29.3 x 52.76in / 74 x 134cm (width x max height)
- **Maximum user weight:** 395lb / 180kg
- **Recline angle:** 90-165° backrest recline
- **Warranty:** 2 years

## Features

- Integrated lumbar support with adjustable positioning
- Premium cushioning throughout the seat and backrest
- Wide range of adjustability options for arms and back
- Well-designed ergonomic seat
- Robust steel chassis construction
- Strong castor wheels
- Easy-to-follow instructions for a smooth build process
- Available in a wide variety of features and finishes

## Pros and Cons

- Wide variety of features
- Very comfortable
- Adjustable lumbar support
- Dimensions and weight tolerance geared for bigger players
- Great-value feature-rich gaming chair
- Logos and stitching are just OK quality
- Lacks a professional or office-chic finish

## Pricing

Priced at approximately $500 / £500, the Kaiser 3 XL offers exceptional bang-for-buck value with its premium feature set and terrific finish.
